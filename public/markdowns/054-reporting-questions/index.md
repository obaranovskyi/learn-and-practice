# Reporting Questions

## Yes/No Questions: if/whether
- "Are you coming?" → She asked **if/whether** I was coming.
- "Do you like it?" → He asked **if** I liked it.

## Wh- Questions: keep the question word
- "Where do you live?" → She asked **where** I lived.
- "What are you doing?" → He asked **what** I was doing.

## Key Changes
- Question word order → Statement word order
- No auxiliary "do" in reported question
- Apply tense backshift

## Examples
- "What time is it?" → She asked what time it **was**.
- "Have you finished?" → He asked if I **had** finished.
