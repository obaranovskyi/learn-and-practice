# Infinitive of Purpose

## Use: Explain why you do something

I went to the store **to buy** milk.
She called **to ask** about the job.
He's saving money **to travel**.

## Alternatives
- **in order to** (more formal)
- **so as to** (formal)
- **so that** + clause

## Examples
- I left early **to avoid** traffic.
- She studied hard **in order to** pass.
- He whispered **so as not to** wake the baby.
- I called **so that** I could confirm.

## Negative: not to / in order not to
- I hid **not to be** seen.
- She left quietly **in order not to** disturb anyone.
