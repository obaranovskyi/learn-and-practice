# Must / Can't / Couldn't (Deduction)

## Logical Deduction

### Must (almost certain)
- She **must** be tired. (I'm sure she is)
- That **must** be John. (99% sure)

### Can't / Couldn't (impossible)
- That **can't** be right! (I'm sure it's wrong)
- He **couldn't** be 50! (seems impossible)

## Present vs Past Deduction

| Time | Positive | Negative |
|------|----------|----------|
| Present | must be | can't/couldn't be |
| Past | must have been | can't/couldn't have been |

## Examples
- She's not answering. She **must** be sleeping.
- He **can't** be at work. It's Sunday!
