# Future with "going to"

Form: **am/is/are + going to + base verb**

## Uses

### 1. Planned Intentions
- I**'m going to study** medicine.
- We**'re going to move** next month.

### 2. Predictions with Evidence
- Look at those clouds! It**'s going to rain**.
- She's going to have a baby. (visible)

## Will vs Going to

| Will | Going to |
|------|----------|
| Spontaneous decision | Planned intention |
| Prediction (opinion) | Prediction (evidence) |
| I'll help! (just decided) | I'm going to help. (planned) |
