# Exercises: Prepositions in Relative Clauses

Rewrite formally (prep + whom/which):

1. The hotel we stayed at was nice.
2. The person I spoke to was helpful.
3. That's the topic we argued about.
4. The chair he sat on was broken.
5. The reason she left for is unclear.

**Answers:** 1. The hotel at which we stayed 2. The person to whom I spoke 3. The topic about which we argued 4. The chair on which he sat 5. The reason for which she left
