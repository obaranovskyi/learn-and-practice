# Exercises: Prepositions of Movement

Fill in the correct preposition:

1. She walked ___ the room. (entered)
2. Drive ___ the bridge. (from one side to other)
3. The cat jumped ___ the wall.
4. We walked ___ the river. (following it)
5. Get ___ the bus at the next stop.

**Answers:** 1. into 2. across/over 3. over 4. along 5. off
