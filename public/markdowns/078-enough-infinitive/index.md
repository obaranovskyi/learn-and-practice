# Adjective + enough + infinitive

## Structure: adjective + enough + to-infinitive

Means: sufficiently [adjective] to do something

## Examples
- She's **old enough to vote**.
- He's **strong enough to lift** it.
- It's **warm enough to go** swimming.

## Word order
- Adjective + **enough**: old enough, good enough
- **Enough** + noun: enough money, enough time

## With "for + object"
- The water is **warm enough for us to swim**.
- Is this **simple enough for you to understand**?
