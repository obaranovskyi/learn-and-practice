# Articles with Countable Nouns

Countable nouns can be singular or plural and follow specific article rules.

## Singular Countable Nouns

Always need a determiner (a/an, the, my, this, etc.):

- ✓ *I need a pen.* / *I need the pen.*
- ✗ *I need pen.*

## Plural Countable Nouns

- **Specific**: Use "the" - *The books on the shelf are mine.*
- **General**: No article - *Books are expensive these days.*

## Article Choice Summary

| Situation | Singular | Plural |
|-----------|----------|--------|
| First mention | a/an | no article or some |
| Known/specific | the | the |
| General statement | a/an (any) | no article |

## Examples

- *A student needs dedication.* (any student)
- *The student I met was friendly.* (specific)
- *Students need dedication.* (all students, general)
