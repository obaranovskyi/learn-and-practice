# Exercises: Articles with Countable Nouns

Add the correct article (a, an, the, or Ø):

1. ___ apple a day keeps ___ doctor away.
2. ___ cars in this city are very expensive.
3. I bought ___ new laptop yesterday.
4. ___ teachers work hard.
5. Where is ___ key I gave you?

**Answers:** 1. An, the 2. Ø (general) or The (specific cars) 3. a 4. Ø 5. the
