# Exercises: Adverbs of Frequency

Put the adverb in the correct position:

1. I go to the gym. (usually)
2. She is on time. (always)
3. They have been to Japan. (never)
4. He works late. (sometimes)
5. We are tired. (often)

**Answers:** 1. I usually go 2. She is always 3. They have never been 4. He sometimes works 5. We are often tired
