# Adverbs of Frequency

Adverbs of frequency tell us **how often** something happens.

## The Frequency Scale

| Adverb | Approximate % |
|--------|---------------|
| always | 100% |
| usually/normally | 90% |
| often/frequently | 70% |
| sometimes | 50% |
| occasionally | 30% |
| rarely/seldom | 10% |
| hardly ever | 5% |
| never | 0% |

## Position

### Before the main verb
- *I **always** eat breakfast.*
- *She **often** visits us.*
- *They **rarely** go out.*

### After "be"
- *He is **always** late.*
- *She is **never** angry.*
- *They are **usually** happy.*

### After auxiliary verbs
- *I have **never** been to Paris.*
- *She can **usually** help.*
- *We will **always** remember.*

## Expressions of Frequency

- *once/twice/three times a day/week/month*
- *every day/week/month/year*
- *from time to time*
- *now and then*
- *every other day* (every 2nd day)
