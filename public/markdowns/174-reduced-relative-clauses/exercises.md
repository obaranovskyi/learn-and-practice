# Exercises: Reduced Relative Clauses

Reduce these clauses:

1. The woman who is sitting there is my aunt.
2. The house which was built in 1900 is historic.
3. Students who study abroad gain experience.
4. The products which are made here are excellent.
5. Anyone who wants to help is welcome.

**Answers:** 1. The woman sitting there 2. The house built in 1900 3. Students studying abroad 4. The products made here 5. Anyone wanting to help
