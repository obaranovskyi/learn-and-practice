# Exercises: Inversion

Rewrite with inversion:

1. I have never seen such a thing.
2. She rarely complains.
3. If I had known, I would have called.
4. She is tired. I am too.
5. He seldom goes out.

**Answers:** 1. Never have I seen such a thing. 2. Rarely does she complain. 3. Had I known, I would have called. 4. So am I. 5. Seldom does he go out.
