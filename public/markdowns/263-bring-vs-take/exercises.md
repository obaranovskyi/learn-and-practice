# Exercises: Bring vs Take

Choose bring or take: 1. ___ me the report. (to speaker) 2. ___ this letter to the post office. (away) 3. Can you ___ wine to my party? (to speaker's event) 4. Don't forget to ___ your keys when you leave. 5. She ___ her dog to my house yesterday.

**Answers:** 1. Bring 2. Take 3. bring 4. take 5. brought
