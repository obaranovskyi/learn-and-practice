# Gerund after Possessive

## Formal: Possessive + gerund
- I appreciate **your helping** me.
- **His leaving** surprised everyone.
- Do you mind **my smoking**?

## Informal: Object pronoun + gerund
- I appreciate **you helping** me.
- Do you mind **me smoking**?

## With nouns
- Formal: **John's arriving** late was annoying.
- Informal: **John arriving** late was annoying.

## When to use
- Formal writing: possessive preferred
- Spoken English: object pronoun common
- Both are grammatically accepted
