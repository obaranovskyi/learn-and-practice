# Present Tenses for Future

## Present Continuous → Fixed Arrangements
- I**'m meeting** John tomorrow. (arranged)
- We**'re flying** to Paris next week. (booked)

## Present Simple → Schedules/Timetables
- The train **leaves** at 9 AM.
- The movie **starts** at 7 PM.

## After time conjunctions (when, after, before, until, as soon as)
- I'll call you **when** I **arrive**. (NOT: when I will arrive)
- **After** she **finishes**, we'll go.
