# Exercises: Subject-Verb Agreement

Choose the correct verb:

1. Everyone (is/are) here.
2. The team (is/are) playing well.
3. Neither she nor they (was/were) ready.
4. The news (is/are) surprising.
5. Bread and butter (is/are) my breakfast.

**Answers:** 1. is 2. is (or are in BrE) 3. were 4. is 5. is
