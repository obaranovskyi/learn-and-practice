# Articles with Proper Nouns

Proper nouns have specific rules for article usage.

## No Article (usually)

- **People's names**: *John, Mary, Dr. Smith*
- **Countries (singular)**: *France, Japan, Brazil*
- **Cities**: *London, Tokyo, New York*
- **Continents**: *Europe, Asia, Africa*
- **Mountains (single)**: *Mount Everest, Mount Fuji*
- **Lakes**: *Lake Victoria, Lake Baikal*
- **Streets**: *Oxford Street, Fifth Avenue*

## Use "the"

- **Countries with plural/republic/kingdom**: *the United States, the UK, the Netherlands*
- **Rivers**: *the Thames, the Amazon, the Nile*
- **Oceans/seas**: *the Pacific, the Mediterranean*
- **Mountain ranges**: *the Alps, the Himalayas*
- **Deserts**: *the Sahara, the Gobi*
- **Newspapers**: *the Times, the Guardian*
- **Famous buildings**: *the Eiffel Tower, the White House*

## Examples

- *She lives in London.* (no article)
- *He crossed the Atlantic.* (ocean → the)
- *They visited the Netherlands.* (plural → the)
