# Exercises: Articles with Proper Nouns

Add "the" where necessary:

1. ___ Mount Everest is in ___ Himalayas.
2. ___ Amazon flows through ___ Brazil.
3. She works at ___ Oxford University.
4. ___ United Kingdom has left ___ European Union.
5. ___ Lake Superior is one of ___ Great Lakes.

**Answers:** 1. Ø, the 2. The, Ø 3. Ø 4. The, the 5. Ø, the
