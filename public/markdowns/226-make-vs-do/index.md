# Make vs Do

## General Rules

**MAKE** = create, produce, construct
**DO** = perform action, complete task

## MAKE

### Creating something
- make breakfast/lunch/dinner
- make coffee/tea
- make a cake

### Producing results
- make money
- make a profit/loss
- make progress

### Decisions & plans
- make a decision/choice
- make a plan
- make an appointment

### Communication
- make a phone call
- make a suggestion
- make a complaint
- make a comment

### Other
- make a mistake
- make friends
- make noise
- make an effort
- make sure

## DO

### Tasks & jobs
- do homework/housework
- do the dishes/laundry
- do a job/work

### General activities
- do business
- do exercise
- do research
- do a course

### Good/bad
- do good/harm
- do damage
- do a favor

### Other
- do your best
- do well/badly
- What do you do? (job)

## Exceptions to Memorize
- do your hair/makeup (prepare appearance)
- make the bed (tidy, not create)
