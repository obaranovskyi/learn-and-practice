# Reporting Commands & Requests

## Form: verb + object + (not) to-infinitive

## Commands: told/ordered
- "Sit down!" → She **told** me **to sit** down.
- "Don't move!" → He **ordered** us **not to move**.

## Requests: asked
- "Please help me." → She **asked** me **to help** her.
- "Could you wait?" → He **asked** me **to wait**.

## Common verbs
- tell, order, command (commands)
- ask, beg, request (requests)
- advise, warn, remind, encourage
