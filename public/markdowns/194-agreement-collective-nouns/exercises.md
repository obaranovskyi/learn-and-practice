# Exercises: Collective Nouns

Choose singular or plural (both may be acceptable):

1. The family (is/are) on vacation.
2. The police (is/are) coming.
3. The team (has/have) won the championship.
4. The news (is/are) shocking.
5. The committee (is/are) divided on the issue.

**Answers:** 1. is (both ok) 2. are 3. has (both ok) 4. is 5. is/are (both ok)
