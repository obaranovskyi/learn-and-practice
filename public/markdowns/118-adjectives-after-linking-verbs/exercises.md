# Exercises: Adjectives After Linking Verbs

Choose adjective or adverb:

1. The music sounds (loud/loudly).
2. She looked at me (angry/angrily).
3. The soup smells (delicious/deliciously).
4. He spoke (quiet/quietly).
5. I feel (bad/badly) about it.

**Answers:** 1. loud 2. angrily 3. delicious 4. quietly 5. bad
