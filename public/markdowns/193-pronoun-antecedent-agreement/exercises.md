# Exercises: Pronoun-Antecedent Agreement

Fill in the correct pronoun:

1. Everyone should do ___ best.
2. The team celebrated ___ victory.
3. Neither girl brought ___ book.
4. Both students finished ___ work.
5. Each person must make ___ own decision.

**Answers:** 1. their/his or her 2. its 3. her 4. their 5. their/his or her
