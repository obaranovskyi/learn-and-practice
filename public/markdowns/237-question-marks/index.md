# Question Marks

Question marks (?) end direct questions.

## Direct Questions
- *What time is it?*
- *Where are you going?*
- *Did you finish?*

## Tag Questions
- *You're coming, aren't you?*
- *She's nice, isn't she?*

## Indirect Questions (NO question mark)
- ✅ *I wonder what time it is.*
- ❌ *I wonder what time it is?*
- ✅ *She asked where I was going.*
- ❌ *She asked where I was going?*

## Polite Requests (optional)
- *Could you help me?* (question)
- *Could you please help me.* (polite command - period OK)

## Question Mark with Quotes

If question is inside quote:
- *She asked, "Where are you?"*

If question is outside quote:
- *Did she say "yes"?*

## Series of Questions
- *What? When? How?*
- *Who did it? Why? When?*

## Uncertainty (in parentheses)
- *He was born in 1920(?).*
- *The author (Shakespeare?) wrote this.*

## NOT for These

Statements with questioning tone use period:
- ✅ *I wonder if she'll come.*
- ❌ *I wonder if she'll come?*
