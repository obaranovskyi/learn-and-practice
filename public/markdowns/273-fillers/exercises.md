# Exercises: Fillers

Identify function: 1. "Well, it's complicated." 2. "I mean, I didn't expect it." 3. "Um... let me think." 4. "So, basically, we need help." 5. "It's, like, really important."

**Answers:** 1. Introducing response 2. Clarifying 3. Buying time 4. Summarizing 5. Approximating/softening
