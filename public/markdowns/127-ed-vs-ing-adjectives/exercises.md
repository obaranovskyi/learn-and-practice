# Exercises: -ed vs -ing Adjectives

Choose the correct form:

1. The lecture was (bored/boring).
2. I'm really (interested/interesting) in history.
3. The news was quite (shocked/shocking).
4. She felt (embarrassed/embarrassing).
5. It's an (excited/exciting) opportunity.

**Answers:** 1. boring 2. interested 3. shocking 4. embarrassed 5. exciting
