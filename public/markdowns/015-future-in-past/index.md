# Future in the Past

Talking about the future from a past perspective.

## Was/Were Going To
- I **was going to** call you, but I forgot.
- She **was going to** come, but she got sick.

## Would (past of will)
- He said he **would** help.
- I knew it **would** rain.

## Use in Reported Speech
- "I will come" → She said she **would** come.
- "I'm going to leave" → He said he **was going to** leave.
