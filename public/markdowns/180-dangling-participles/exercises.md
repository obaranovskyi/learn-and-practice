# Exercises: Dangling Participles

Correct the dangling participles:

1. Running for the bus, my bag fell.
2. Having eaten dinner, the movie started.
3. Tired after work, a nap was needed.
4. Reading the book, it was very interesting.
5. Driving home, a deer was seen.

**Answers:** 1. Running for the bus, I dropped my bag. 2. Having eaten dinner, we started the movie. 3. Tired after work, she needed a nap. 4. Reading the book, I found it interesting. 5. Driving home, I saw a deer.
