# Reporting that-clauses

## Optional "that"
- He said **(that)** he was tired.
- She told me **(that)** she would come.

## Verbs with that-clauses
- say, tell, explain, mention, complain
- think, believe, know, realize, understand
- hope, expect, promise

## Examples
- She **explained that** the train was late.
- He **mentioned that** he had met her before.
- I **believe that** he's telling the truth.

## Note
"That" is more common in formal writing.
