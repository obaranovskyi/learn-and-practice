# Zero Article (No Article)

Sometimes no article is needed. This is called the **zero article**.

## When to Use Zero Article

1. **Plural countable nouns (general)**: *Dogs are loyal.* *Books are expensive.*
2. **Uncountable nouns (general)**: *Water is essential.* *Music relaxes me.*
3. **Names**: *John, London, France*
4. **Languages**: *She speaks Spanish.*
5. **Meals**: *Have breakfast, eat lunch, make dinner*
6. **Sports/games**: *play football, chess*
7. **Academic subjects**: *study history, learn mathematics*
8. **Days, months, seasons (general)**: *on Monday, in June, in winter*
9. **Bed, work, home, school** (purpose): *go to bed, at work, go home*

## Examples

- *Life is beautiful.* (general concept)
- *Children need love.* (all children, general)
- *He goes to school.* (for education)
- *She's at work.* (working)
