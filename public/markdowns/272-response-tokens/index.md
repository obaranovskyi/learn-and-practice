# Response Tokens

Small words showing you're listening and engaged.

## Showing Interest

- *Oh!* - surprise
- *Really?* - interest/mild surprise
- *Oh really?* - more surprise
- *Wow!* - strong surprise
- *No way!* - disbelief
- *Seriously?* - seeking confirmation

## Acknowledging

- *I see.* - understanding
- *Right.* - understanding/agreement
- *OK.* - acceptance
- *Uh-huh.* / *Mm-hmm.* - listening
- *Yeah.* - agreement/listening

## Expressing Sympathy

- *Oh no!* - bad news
- *Oh dear!* - concern
- *That's terrible!* - strong sympathy
- *I'm sorry to hear that.*
- *How awful!*

## Expressing Approval

- *Great!* / *Wonderful!*
- *How nice!* / *Lovely!*
- *Good for you!*
- *That's amazing!*

## Encouraging Speaker to Continue

- *And then?*
- *Go on...*
- *What happened next?*
- *Tell me more.*

## Common Patterns

| News | Response |
|------|----------|
| Good news | Oh, that's great! |
| Bad news | Oh no, I'm sorry. |
| Surprising | Really? Wow! |
| Neutral | I see. Right. |

## Why Important?

- Shows active listening
- Builds rapport
- Keeps conversation flowing
- Silence can seem rude
