# Exercises: Response Tokens

Match appropriate response: 1. "I got the job!" 2. "My car broke down." 3. "I'm moving to Japan." 4. "So I told him..." (continuing story) 5. "It costs $5000."

**Answers:** 1. Great!/Congratulations! 2. Oh no!/That's terrible! 3. Really?/Wow! 4. Uh-huh/And then? 5. I see./Right.
