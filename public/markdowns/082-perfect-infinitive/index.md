# Perfect Infinitive

## Form: to have + past participle

## Uses

### With modals (past reference)
- She must **have left**. (deduction)
- He should **have called**. (criticism)
- I could **have helped**. (unused ability)

### After seem/appear
- He seems **to have forgotten**.
- She appeared **to have left**.

### After claim/pretend
- He claims **to have met** the president.
- She pretended **to have finished**.

### Expressing unfulfilled intention
- I hoped **to have finished** by now.
- I meant **to have called** you.
