# Exercises: Much/Many/A lot of

Fill in much, many, or a lot of:

1. Do you have ___ free time?
2. There are ___ cars on the road.
3. She doesn't have ___ patience.
4. How ___ books did you read?
5. We have ___ options to choose from.

**Answers:** 1. much 2. many/a lot of 3. much 4. many 5. a lot of/many/plenty of
