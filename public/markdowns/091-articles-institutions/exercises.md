# Exercises: Articles with Institutions

Add "the" where necessary:

1. She goes to ___ church every Sunday.
2. ___ school near my house has 500 students.
3. He's in ___ hospital recovering from surgery.
4. I'll visit ___ university tomorrow for a tour.
5. The criminal was sent to ___ prison for 5 years.

**Answers:** 1. Ø 2. The 3. Ø (BrE) or the (AmE) 4. the 5. Ø
