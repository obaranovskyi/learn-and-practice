# Exercises: Business English

Make diplomatic: 1. "You're wrong." 2. "That's a bad idea." 3. "I don't have time." 4. "What do you want?" 5. "No, we can't do that."

**Answers:** 1. I see your point, however... 2. That's an interesting idea, but have you considered...? 3. I don't have the bandwidth for that currently. 4. What can I help you with? 5. Unfortunately, that may not be possible at this time.
