# Exercises: Not As...As

Rewrite using "not as...as":

1. This book is shorter than that one.
   This book is ___.
2. Monday was colder than Tuesday.
   Tuesday was ___.
3. My car is older than yours.
   Your car is ___.
4. The exam was easier than I expected.
   The exam was ___.
5. He speaks more quietly than his sister.
   He doesn't speak ___.

**Answers:** 1. not as long as that one 2. not as cold as Monday 3. not as old as mine 4. not as difficult as I expected 5. as loudly as his sister
