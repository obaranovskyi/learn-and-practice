# Exercises: Because/As/Since

Fill in because, as, or since:

1. ___ you asked, I'll tell you.
2. I can't go ___ I'm busy.
3. ___ it was cold, we lit a fire.
4. ___ you're here, help me with this.
5. Why are you late? ___ I missed the bus.

**Answers:** 1. Since/As 2. because 3. As/Since 4. Since 5. Because
