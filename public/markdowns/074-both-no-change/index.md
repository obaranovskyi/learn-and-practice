# Verbs taking both (no meaning change)

## Same meaning with -ing or to-infinitive

### Like, Love, Hate, Prefer
- I **like swimming** / I **like to swim**
- She **loves dancing** / She **loves to dance**

### Begin, Start, Continue
- It **started raining** / It **started to rain**
- He **continued working** / He **continued to work**

## Subtle preferences
- **Like doing** = enjoy the activity
- **Like to do** = think it's a good idea

I **like getting** up early. (enjoy it)
I **like to get** up early. (it's sensible)

## Avoid double -ing
❌ It's starting raining.
✅ It's **starting to rain**.
