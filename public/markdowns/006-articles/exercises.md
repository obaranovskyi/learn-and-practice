# Exercises: Articles

## Exercise 1: A, An, or The?

1. I saw ____ interesting movie last night.
2. ____ movie was about a young scientist.
3. She's ____ honest person.
4. He works at ____ university.
5. ____ sun is shining brightly today.

**Answers:**
1. an
2. The
3. an
4. a
5. The

---

## Exercise 2: Article or No Article?

Fill in the blank with a, an, the, or ∅ (nothing).

1. I love ____ Italian food.
2. ____ Italian restaurant on Main Street is excellent.
3. She plays ____ violin beautifully.
4. He plays ____ basketball every weekend.
5. I need to go to ____ bank today.

**Answers:**
1. ∅ (general)
2. The (specific)
3. the (musical instrument)
4. ∅ (sport)
5. the (specific, known from context)

## Exercise 3: Fill in All the Blanks

(1)____ young woman walked into (2)____ room. She was carrying (3)____ umbrella and (4)____ bag. (5)____ bag was full of (6)____ books. She sat down in (7)____ corner and started reading (8)____ book about (9)____ history of (10)____ Europe.

**Answers:**
1. A
2. the
3. an
4. a
5. The
6. ∅
7. a/the
8. a
9. the
10. ∅

---

## Exercise 4: Correct the Mistakes

1. He's a engineer.
2. She loves the music.
3. I need an advice.
4. The Paris is beautiful.
5. He plays the football.

**Answers:**
1. He's **an** engineer.
2. She loves ∅ music.
3. I need ∅ advice. (or: a piece of advice)
4. ∅ Paris is beautiful.
5. He plays ∅ football.

---

## Exercise 5: Choose the Correct Option

1. She's (a / an / the) European.
2. I had (a / an / the) hour to wait.
3. (A / An / The) moon is bright tonight.
4. He wants to be (a / an / the) doctor.
5. We visited (a / an / the) United States.

**Answers:**
1. a (sounds like "you")
2. an (silent h)
3. The (unique)
4. a (profession)
5. the (country with plural name)
