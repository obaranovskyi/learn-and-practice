# Prepositions of Place

## At, On, In

| Preposition | Use | Examples |
|-------------|-----|----------|
| **at** | specific point | at the bus stop, at home, at work |
| **on** | surface, line | on the table, on the wall, on a street |
| **in** | enclosed space | in the room, in the box, in London |

## Position Prepositions

| Preposition | Meaning | Example |
|-------------|---------|---------|
| above | higher than | The picture is above the sofa. |
| over | directly above | There's a bridge over the river. |
| below | lower than | The temperature is below zero. |
| under | directly beneath | The cat is under the table. |
| behind | at the back of | Park behind the building. |
| in front of | facing | Stand in front of the camera. |
| beside/next to | at the side | Sit beside me. |
| between | in the middle of two | between London and Paris |
| among | surrounded by many | among the trees |
| near/close to | not far | near the station |
| opposite | facing, across | opposite the bank |

## Common Expressions

- *at home, at work, at school*
- *in bed, in hospital, in prison*
- *on the bus/train/plane* (public transport)
- *in the car/taxi* (private vehicle)
