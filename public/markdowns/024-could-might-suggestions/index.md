# Could / Might (Suggestions)

## Could (tentative suggestions)
- You **could** try the new restaurant.
- We **could** go to the movies.

## Might (very tentative)
- You **might** want to check again. (gentle)
- It **might** be worth trying.

## Could vs Should
- **Could** = option/possibility
- **Should** = advice/recommendation

"You **could** take a taxi" (option)
"You **should** take a taxi" (advice)
