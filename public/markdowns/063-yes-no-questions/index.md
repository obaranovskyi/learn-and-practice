# Yes/No Questions

Form: **Auxiliary + Subject + Main verb...?**

## Examples by tense
- **Do** you like coffee?
- **Does** she work here?
- **Did** they arrive?
- **Is** he coming?
- **Have** you finished?
- **Can** you help?
- **Will** they come?

## Short answers
- Yes, I **do**. / No, I **don't**.
- Yes, she **is**. / No, she **isn't**.
- Yes, we **have**. / No, we **haven't**.
