# Object Pronouns

Object pronouns receive the action of the verb or follow prepositions.

## Object Pronouns

| Subject | Object |
|---------|--------|
| I | me |
| you | you |
| he | him |
| she | her |
| it | it |
| we | us |
| they | them |

## Usage

### After Verbs (Direct Object)
- *She loves **him**.*
- *I saw **them** yesterday.*
- *Can you help **me**?*

### After Verbs (Indirect Object)
- *Give **me** the book.*
- *Tell **her** the truth.*
- *I bought **them** presents.*

### After Prepositions
- *Come with **me**.*
- *This is for **you**.*
- *She sat next to **him**.*

## Common Mistakes

- ✗ *Between you and I*
- ✓ *Between you and me* (after preposition)

- ✗ *She gave it to he and I*
- ✓ *She gave it to him and me*
