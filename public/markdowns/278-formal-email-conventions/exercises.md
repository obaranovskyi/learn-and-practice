# Exercises: Formal Email

Make formal: 1. I want to know about... 2. Can you send me...? 3. Here's the file. 4. Get back to me. 5. Thanks.

**Answers:** 1. I am writing to inquire about... 2. I would be grateful if you could send me... 3. Please find attached... 4. I look forward to hearing from you. 5. Thank you for your consideration.
