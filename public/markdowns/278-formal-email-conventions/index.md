# Formal Letter/Email Conventions

Standard phrases and structures for professional correspondence.

## Salutations

- *Dear Mr./Ms./Dr. [Last name],* (known name)
- *Dear Sir or Madam,* (unknown)
- *To Whom It May Concern,* (very formal)
- *Dear Hiring Manager,* (job application)

## Opening Lines

- *I am writing to inquire about...*
- *I am writing with reference to...*
- *Further to our conversation,...*
- *Thank you for your email of [date].*
- *I hope this email finds you well.*

## Making Requests

- *I would be grateful if you could...*
- *Could you please...*
- *I would appreciate it if...*
- *Would it be possible to...*

## Giving Information

- *Please find attached...*
- *I am pleased to inform you...*
- *I regret to inform you...*
- *I would like to draw your attention to...*

## Closing Lines

- *Please do not hesitate to contact me if...*
- *I look forward to hearing from you.*
- *Thank you for your consideration.*
- *Should you require any further information,...*

## Sign-offs

- *Yours sincerely,* (if you know the name)
- *Yours faithfully,* (if you don't know the name - British)
- *Best regards,* / *Kind regards,*
- *Respectfully,* (very formal)

## Avoid

- Contractions (I'm, don't)
- Slang/colloquialisms
- Emojis
- ALL CAPS
- Exclamation marks (mostly)
