# Exercises: Apposition

Add commas if needed: 1. My sister Jane is a doctor. 2. Paris the capital of France is beautiful. 3. The poet Shakespeare wrote many plays. 4. Einstein a famous scientist won the Nobel Prize. 5. The city of Rome is ancient.

**Answers:** 1. No commas (restrictive) 2. Paris, the capital of France, is beautiful. 3. No commas (title + name) 4. Einstein, a famous scientist, won... 5. No commas (of-phrase)
