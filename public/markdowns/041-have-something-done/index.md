# Have Something Done (Causative)

Form: **have + object + past participle**

## Meaning
Someone else does something for you (service).

## Examples
- I **had my hair cut**. (hairdresser cut it)
- She **had her car repaired**. (mechanic repaired it)
- We're **having our house painted**. (painters are doing it)

## Tenses
- Present: I **have** my car washed.
- Past: I **had** my car washed.
- Future: I **will have** my car washed.
- Perfect: I **have had** my car washed.

## Also used for negative experiences
- He **had his wallet stolen**. (misfortune)
- She **had her heart broken**.
