# May / Might / Could (Possibility)

## Present/Future Possibility

### May (~50%)
- It **may** rain tomorrow.
- She **may** be at home.

### Might (~30-40%)
- It **might** rain. (slightly less certain)
- He **might** come later.

### Could (~30%)
- She **could** be right.
- That **could** be true.

## Negative
- It **may not** rain.
- She **might not** come.
- **Couldn't** = impossibility (not possibility)

## Certainty Scale
will (100%) → must (95%) → may (50%) → might/could (30-40%)
