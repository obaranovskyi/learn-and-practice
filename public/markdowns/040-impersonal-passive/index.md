# Impersonal Passive

## Two structures:

### 1. It + passive + that-clause
- **It is said that** he is rich.
- **It is believed that** she left.
- **It was reported that** they escaped.

### 2. Subject + passive + infinitive
- **He is said to be** rich.
- **She is believed to have left**.
- **They were reported to have escaped**.

## Common verbs
say, believe, think, know, report, consider, expect, allege, claim, understand

## Use
- Formal writing, news reports
- When source is unspecified
