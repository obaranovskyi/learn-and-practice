# Exercises: Adverbs of Time

Fill in an appropriate time adverb:

1. I'll call you ___ (near future).
2. She arrived ___ (a short time before now).
3. We met two years ___.
4. ___, I don't have time.
5. The package will arrive ___ (the day after today).

**Answers:** 1. soon/later 2. recently/just 3. ago 4. Currently/Now 5. tomorrow
