# Future Continuous

Actions in progress at a specific future time. Form: **will be + verb-ing**

## Uses

### 1. Action in Progress at Future Time
- At 8 PM, I **will be watching** TV.
- This time tomorrow, I**'ll be flying** to Paris.

### 2. Polite Questions
- **Will** you **be using** the car tonight?

### 3. Planned/Expected Events
- I**'ll be seeing** John tomorrow. (already arranged)
