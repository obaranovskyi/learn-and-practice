# Exercises: Present Participles

Identify the use of the -ing form:

1. She is reading a book. ___
2. The crying baby woke us up. ___
3. I heard him laughing. ___
4. Walking to work, I met John. ___
5. Running is good exercise. ___

**Answers:** 1. continuous tense 2. adjective 3. after perception verb 4. participle clause 5. gerund (noun)
