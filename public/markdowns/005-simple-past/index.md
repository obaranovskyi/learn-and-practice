# Simple Past Tense

The Simple Past describes completed actions at a specific time in the past.

## Form

### Regular Verbs: Add -ed
- work → worked
- play → played
- study → studied

### Irregular Verbs: Memorize!
- go → went
- see → saw
- have → had

### Negative
**Subject + did not (didn't) + base verb**
- I **didn't work** yesterday.
- She **didn't go** to the party.

### Question
**Did + subject + base verb?**
- **Did** you **work** yesterday?
- **Did** she **go** to the party?

## Uses

### 1. Completed Actions at a Specific Time
- I **visited** Paris last summer.
- She **called** me yesterday.
- They **moved** here in 2015.

### 2. Sequence of Past Actions
- I **woke up**, **had** breakfast, and **left** for work.
- She **opened** the door and **walked** in.

### 3. Past Habits (often with "used to")
- I **played** tennis when I was young.
- We **lived** in London for five years.

### 4. Past Facts/Situations
- He **was** a teacher for 20 years.
- The Romans **built** many roads.

## Spelling Rules for Regular Verbs

| Verb ending | Rule | Example |
|-------------|------|---------|
| Most verbs | Add **-ed** | work → worked |
| -e | Add **-d** | live → lived |
| Consonant + y | Change y to **-ied** | study → studied |
| Vowel + y | Add **-ed** | play → played |
| Short vowel + consonant | Double + **-ed** | stop → stopped |

## Common Irregular Verbs

| Base | Past | Base | Past |
|------|------|------|------|
| be | was/were | go | went |
| have | had | see | saw |
| do | did | come | came |
| say | said | make | made |
| get | got | know | knew |
| take | took | give | gave |
| think | thought | find | found |
| tell | told | leave | left |
| buy | bought | eat | ate |
| write | wrote | read | read |

## Time Expressions

- yesterday, last night/week/month/year
- ago (two days ago, a week ago)
- in + year (in 2010, in the 1990s)
- when I was young
- at that time, then

## Common Mistakes

❌ I didn't went there.  
✅ I didn't **go** there.

❌ Did you went?  
✅ Did you **go**?

❌ I was work yesterday.  
✅ I **worked** yesterday.

❌ She study hard last night.  
✅ She **studied** hard last night.
