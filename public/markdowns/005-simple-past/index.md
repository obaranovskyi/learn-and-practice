# Simple Past

Describes completed actions at a specific past time.

## Form

- **Regular:** verb + ed (worked, played)
- **Irregular:** memorize (go→went, see→saw)
- **Negative:** didn't + base verb
- **Question:** Did + subject + base verb?

## Common Irregular Verbs

| Base | Past | Base | Past |
|------|------|------|------|
| be | was/were | go | went |
| have | had | see | saw |
| do | did | come | came |
| take | took | make | made |

## Time Expressions
yesterday, last week, ago, in 2010, when I was young

## Common Mistakes

❌ I didn't went → ✅ I didn't **go**
❌ Did you went? → ✅ Did you **go**?
