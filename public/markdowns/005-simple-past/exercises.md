# Exercises: Simple Past

## Exercise 1: Regular or Irregular?
Write the past form.

1. work → ______
2. go → ______
3. study → ______
4. see → ______
5. stop → ______
6. have → ______
7. live → ______
8. buy → ______

**Answers:**
1. worked
2. went
3. studied
4. saw
5. stopped
6. had
7. lived
8. bought

---

## Exercise 2: Fill in the Blanks

1. She ______ (visit) her grandmother last weekend.
2. They ______ (not/come) to the party.
3. ______ you ______ (see) the movie?
4. I ______ (wake up) late this morning.
5. He ______ (be) tired after work.

**Answers:**
1. visited
2. didn't come
3. Did, see
4. woke up
5. was

---

## Exercise 3: Correct the Mistakes

1. I didn't saw him yesterday.
2. She goed to school by bus.
3. Did they came on time?
4. He not finished his homework.
5. We was at home last night.

**Answers:**
1. I didn't **see** him yesterday.
2. She **went** to school by bus.
3. Did they **come** on time?
4. He **didn't finish** his homework.
5. We **were** at home last night.

---

## Exercise 4: Make Questions

Turn into questions.

1. She called you.
2. They arrived late.
3. He bought a new car.
4. You finished the project.
5. The movie started at 8.

**Answers:**
1. Did she call you?
2. Did they arrive late?
3. Did he buy a new car?
4. Did you finish the project?
5. Did the movie start at 8?

---

## Exercise 5: Write a Short Story

Put the verbs in Simple Past to complete the story.

Yesterday, I (1)______ (wake up) at 7 AM. I (2)______ (have) breakfast and (3)______ (leave) for work. On the way, I (4)______ (see) my old friend. We (5)______ (talk) for a few minutes. Then I (6)______ (arrive) at work and (7)______ (start) my day.

**Answers:**
1. woke up
2. had
3. left
4. saw
5. talked
6. arrived
7. started
