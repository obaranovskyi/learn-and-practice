# Exercises: Simple Past

## Exercise 1: Write the past form

1. work → ______ 2. go → ______ 3. see → ______ 4. have → ______

**Answers:** 1. worked 2. went 3. saw 4. had

---

## Exercise 2: Complete

1. She ______ (visit) Paris last year.
2. They ______ (not/come) to the party.
3. ______ you ______ (see) the movie?

**Answers:** 1. visited 2. didn't come 3. Did, see
