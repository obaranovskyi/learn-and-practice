# Exercises: Neither...nor

Complete with neither...nor:

1. ___ he ___ she knows the answer.
2. I speak ___ French ___ German.
3. ___ the bus ___ the train was on time.
4. She has ___ patience ___ tolerance.
5. ___ John ___ his brothers were there.

**Answers:** 1. Neither, nor 2. neither, nor 3. Neither, nor 4. neither, nor 5. Neither, nor
