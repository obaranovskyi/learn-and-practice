# Neither...nor

"Neither...nor" negates two things.

## Basic Usage

- ***Neither** coffee **nor** tea was served.*
- *He speaks **neither** English **nor** French.*
- *I have **neither** the time **nor** the money.*

## Meaning

Not one and not the other:
- ***Neither** John **nor** Mary came.* (not John, not Mary)
- *The movie was **neither** funny **nor** interesting.*

## Subject-Verb Agreement

Verb agrees with the **nearer** subject:
- ***Neither** he **nor** I **am** going.*
- ***Neither** the students **nor** the teacher **was** ready.*
- ***Neither** she **nor** they **have** arrived.*

## "Neither" Alone

- ***Neither** option is good.* (not one, not the other)
- *I like **neither** of them.*
- *Neither do I.* (= I don't either)

## Double Negatives - Avoid!

- ✗ *I don't like neither.* (double negative)
- ✓ *I like neither.* / *I don't like either.*

## Comparison with Either...or

| Positive Choice | Negative |
|-----------------|----------|
| either...or | neither...nor |
| *Either John or Mary* | *Neither John nor Mary* |
