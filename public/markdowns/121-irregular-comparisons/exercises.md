# Exercises: Irregular Comparisons

Fill in the correct form:

1. This is ___ (good) than that.
2. Today is the ___ (bad) day ever.
3. I need ___ (far) information.
4. She's my ___ (old) sister.
5. He has ___ (little) money than me.

**Answers:** 1. better 2. worst 3. further 4. elder/older 5. less
