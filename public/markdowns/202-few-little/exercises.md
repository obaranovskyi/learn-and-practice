# Exercises: Few/A few/Little/A little

Choose the correct option:

1. I have (few/a few) good friends. I'm happy.
2. There's (little/a little) hope left. It's almost hopeless.
3. Can I have (few/a few) more minutes?
4. (Few/A few) students passed. Most failed.
5. I'd like (little/a little) sugar in my tea.

**Answers:** 1. a few 2. little 3. a few 4. Few 5. a little
