# Bare Infinitive (without "to")

## After modals
- I **can swim**.
- She **will come**.
- You **must go**.

## After let/make/have (causative)
- **Let** me **help**.
- **Make** him **stay**.
- **Have** them **wait**.

## After perception verbs
- I saw her **leave**.
- I heard him **sing**.
- I watched them **play**.

## After "had better" and "would rather"
- You **had better go**.
- I **would rather stay**.

## After "why (not)"
- **Why not try** again?
- **Why wait**?

## Note: Passive changes to to-infinitive
Active: I saw her **leave**.
Passive: She was seen **to leave**.
