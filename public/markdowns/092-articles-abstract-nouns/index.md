# Articles with Abstract Nouns

Abstract nouns (concepts, feelings, qualities) follow specific article rules.

## No Article (General Concept)

When speaking about the concept in general:

- *Love is powerful.*
- *Happiness comes from within.*
- *Time flies.*
- *Knowledge is important.*
- *Life is short.*

## Use "the" (Specific Instance)

When referring to a specific instance or modified concept:

- *The love I have for you is eternal.*
- *The happiness she felt was overwhelming.*
- *The time we spent together was precious.*
- *The knowledge gained from experience is valuable.*

## Common Abstract Nouns

- **Feelings**: love, hate, fear, joy, happiness, sadness
- **Concepts**: time, life, death, nature, beauty
- **Qualities**: honesty, courage, patience, wisdom
- **Ideas**: freedom, justice, truth, education

## Examples

- *Education is a right.* (general)
- *The education I received was excellent.* (specific)
- *Beauty is subjective.* (general)
- *The beauty of this place amazed me.* (specific)
