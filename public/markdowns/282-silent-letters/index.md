# Silent Letters

Letters written but not pronounced.

## Silent K

Before N:
- **k**nife, **k**now, **k**nee, **k**nock, **k**night, **k**not

## Silent W

Before R:
- **w**rite, **w**rong, **w**rap, **w**rist, **w**reck

In some words:
- ans**w**er, s**w**ord, t**w**o

## Silent G

Before N:
- **g**nat, **g**nome, si**g**n, desi**g**n, forei**g**n

## Silent B

After M:
- clim**b**, com**b**, lam**b**, thum**b**, bom**b**, dum**b**

Before T:
- dou**b**t, de**b**t, su**b**tle

## Silent H

- **h**onest, **h**our, **h**onor, **h**eir
- w**h**at, w**h**en (varies by accent)
- g**h**ost, r**h**ythm

## Silent L

- ca**l**m, pa**l**m, sa**l**mon, wa**l**k, ta**l**k
- cou**l**d, wou**l**d, shou**l**d

## Silent P

- **p**sychology, **p**neumonia, **p**seudo
- recei**p**t

## Silent T

- cas**t**le, lis**t**en, of**t**en (varies)
- Chris**t**mas, mor**t**gage

## Silent GH

- thou**gh**, throu**gh**, ni**gh**t, li**gh**t
- wei**gh**, nei**gh**bor

## Tips

Many silent letters are historical - spelling preserved older pronunciations.
