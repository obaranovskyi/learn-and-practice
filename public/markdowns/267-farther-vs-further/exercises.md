# Exercises: Farther vs Further

Choose farther or further: 1. We need ___ information. 2. The beach is ___ than the park. 3. Let's not discuss this ___. 4. Without ___ delay, let's begin. 5. How much ___ is it to the station?

**Answers:** 1. further 2. farther 3. further 4. further 5. farther (or further)
