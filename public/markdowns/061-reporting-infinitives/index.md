# Reporting with Infinitives

## Verbs + to-infinitive
agree, decide, offer, promise, refuse, threaten

- "I'll help." → He **agreed to help**.
- "I won't go." → She **refused to go**.

## Verbs + object + to-infinitive
advise, ask, beg, encourage, invite, order, remind, tell, warn

- "Don't touch it!" → He **warned me not to touch** it.
- "Please come." → She **invited me to come**.

## Examples
- "I'll pay." → He **offered to pay**.
- "Study hard." → She **advised me to study** hard.
