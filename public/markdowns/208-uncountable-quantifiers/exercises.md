# Exercises: Uncountable Quantifiers

Choose the correct quantifier:

1. Is there (many/much) traffic today?
2. I have (a little/a few) time to help.
3. (Little/Few) information is available.
4. How (many/much) advice did she give?
5. A great deal of (work/works) is needed.

**Answers:** 1. much 2. a little 3. Little 4. much 5. work
