# Exercises: Present Perfect Continuous

## Exercise 1: Complete

1. She ______ (wait) for 2 hours.
2. How long ______ you ______ (study)?
3. It ______ (rain) all day.

**Answers:** 1. has been waiting 2. have, been studying 3. has been raining

---

## Exercise 2: Simple or Continuous?

1. I ______ (read) this book for 3 hours. (still reading)
2. I ______ (read) 3 books this month. (quantity)

**Answers:** 1. have been reading 2. have read
