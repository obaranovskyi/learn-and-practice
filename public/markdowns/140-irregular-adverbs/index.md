# Irregular Adverbs

Some adverbs don't follow the regular -ly pattern.

## Same Form as Adjective

| Word | As Adjective | As Adverb |
|------|--------------|-----------|
| fast | a fast car | drive fast |
| hard | hard work | work hard |
| late | late arrival | arrive late |
| early | early bird | come early |
| straight | straight line | go straight |
| daily | daily news | published daily |
| weekly | weekly meeting | meets weekly |

## Different Meaning with -ly

| Without -ly | With -ly |
|-------------|----------|
| hard (with effort) | hardly (barely) |
| late (not on time) | lately (recently) |
| near (close) | nearly (almost) |
| high (elevation) | highly (very much) |
| deep (physically) | deeply (emotionally) |
| wide (physical width) | widely (extensively) |
| free (no cost) | freely (without restriction) |

## Examples

- *Work **hard**!* vs *I **hardly** know him.*
- *He came **late**.* vs *I've been busy **lately**.*
- *Aim **high**.* vs *It's **highly** unlikely.*
- *Open it **wide**.* vs *It's **widely** known.*

## "Well" as Adverb of "Good"

- *She's a **good** singer.* (adjective)
- *She sings **well**.* (adverb)
