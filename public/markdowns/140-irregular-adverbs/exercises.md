# Exercises: Irregular Adverbs

Choose the correct form:

1. She (hard/hardly) ever calls me.
2. The plane flew (high/highly) above the clouds.
3. This information is (high/highly) confidential.
4. He arrived (late/lately).
5. Have you seen her (late/lately)?

**Answers:** 1. hardly 2. high 3. highly 4. late 5. lately
