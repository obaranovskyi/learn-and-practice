# Reporting Verbs

## Verb + -ing
- admit, deny, suggest, recommend
- He **admitted stealing** the money.
- She **suggested going** out.

## Verb + to-infinitive
- agree, offer, promise, refuse, threaten
- He **agreed to help**.
- She **refused to answer**.

## Verb + object + to-infinitive
- advise, warn, remind, encourage, invite
- She **advised me to wait**.
- He **warned us not to go**.

## Verb + that-clause
- explain, mention, complain, insist
- She **explained that** she was late.
