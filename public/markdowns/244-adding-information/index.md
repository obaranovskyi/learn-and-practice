# Adding Information

Words and phrases that add related ideas.

## Formal Connectors

- **Furthermore**: *The plan is effective. Furthermore, it's affordable.*
- **Moreover**: *She's talented. Moreover, she's hardworking.*
- **In addition**: *He speaks French. In addition, he knows German.*
- **Additionally**: *Additionally, we need more funding.*
- **Besides**: *Besides being cheap, it's reliable.*

## Informal Connectors

- **Also**: *She's smart. She's also kind.*
- **Too/As well**: *I like coffee. I like tea too/as well.*
- **And**: *He's tall and strong.*
- **Plus**: *It's cheap. Plus, it's good quality.*

## Coordinating

- **Not only... but also**: *She's not only smart but also creative.*
- **Both... and**: *Both the price and quality are good.*
- **As well as**: *She speaks French as well as German.*

## Position

- **Furthermore/Moreover**: beginning of sentence
- **Also**: various positions
  - *She also speaks French.*
  - *Also, she speaks French.*
- **Too/As well**: end of sentence
  - *I like it too.* / *I like it as well.*
