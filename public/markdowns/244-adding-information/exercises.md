# Exercises: Adding Information

Choose: furthermore, also, in addition, not only... but also: 1. She's smart. ___, she's kind. (formal) 2. He ___ speaks French. (informal) 3. ___ to English, she speaks German. 4. She's ___ talented ___ hardworking.

**Answers:** 1. Furthermore 2. also 3. In addition 4. not only... but also
