# Compound Words

Compound words combine two or more words.

## Types by Spelling

### Closed (one word)
- football, bookshelf, homework
- bedroom, toothpaste, sunlight

### Hyphenated
- mother-in-law, well-known, self-confident
- part-time, up-to-date

### Open (two words)
- ice cream, post office, high school
- living room, bus stop

## Types by Word Class

### Noun + Noun
- toothbrush, bedroom, sunshine

### Adjective + Noun
- blackboard, greenhouse, fast food

### Verb + Noun
- swimming pool, washing machine

### Noun + Verb
- sunrise, haircut

### Other combinations
- overcome (over + verb)
- understand (under + verb)

## Stress Pattern

Usually stressed on the **first** element:
- **BOOK**shelf, **FOOT**ball, **SUN**light
