# Exercises: Adverb Position

Put the adverb in the correct position:

1. She speaks French. (fluently)
2. I have been to China. (never)
3. He is happy. (always)
4. We'll meet. (probably)
5. She danced at the party. (beautifully / last night)

**Answers:** 1. She speaks French fluently. 2. I have never been 3. He is always happy 4. We'll probably meet 5. She danced beautifully at the party last night.
