# Exercises: Lay vs Lie

Choose lay/lie (correct form): 1. Please ___ the books on the desk. (present) 2. I need to ___ down. (present) 3. She ___ on the beach yesterday. (past) 4. He ___ the baby in the crib. (past) 5. The cat is ___ on the sofa. (present progressive)

**Answers:** 1. lay 2. lie 3. lay 4. laid 5. lying
