# Get Something Done (Causative)

Form: **get + object + past participle**

## Similar to "have something done" but:
- More informal
- Often implies effort/difficulty
- More common in American English

## Examples
- I need to **get my car fixed**.
- She finally **got her phone repaired**.
- We **got the work finished** on time.

## Have vs Get
| Have | Get |
|------|-----|
| Neutral/formal | Informal |
| Standard service | Implies effort |
| I had my hair cut | I got my hair cut |
