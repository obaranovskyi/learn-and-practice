# Simple Future with "will"

Form: **will + base verb** (won't for negative)

## Uses

### 1. Predictions
- It **will rain** tomorrow.
- She **will be** successful.

### 2. Spontaneous Decisions
- I'm cold. I**'ll close** the window.
- The phone's ringing. I**'ll get** it.

### 3. Promises
- I **will help** you.
- I **won't tell** anyone.

### 4. Offers
- I**'ll carry** that for you.

### 5. Facts About Future
- The sun **will rise** at 6 AM.
