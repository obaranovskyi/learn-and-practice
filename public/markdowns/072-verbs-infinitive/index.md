# Verbs followed by Infinitive (to + verb)

## Common verbs + to-infinitive

### Wanting/Planning
want, need, hope, expect, plan, intend, decide

### Offering/Agreeing
offer, agree, promise, refuse, threaten

### Learning/Trying
learn, manage, fail, afford, tend

### Seeming
seem, appear, pretend

## Examples
- I **want to go** home.
- She **decided to leave**.
- He **agreed to help**.
- They **promised to come**.
