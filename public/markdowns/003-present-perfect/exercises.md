# Exercises: Present Perfect

## Exercise 1: Complete

1. I ______ (never/be) to Australia.
2. She ______ (just/finish) work.
3. They ______ (live) here since 2010.

**Answers:** 1. have never been 2. has just finished 3. have lived

---

## Exercise 2: For or Since?

1. I've known her ______ 3 years.
2. He's worked here ______ January.

**Answers:** 1. for 2. since

---

## Exercise 3: Present Perfect or Simple Past?

1. I ______ (see) him yesterday.
2. She ______ (never/eat) sushi.

**Answers:** 1. saw 2. has never eaten
