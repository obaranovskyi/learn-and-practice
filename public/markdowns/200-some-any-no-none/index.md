# Some, Any, No, None

## Some

**Positive** sentences and offers/requests:
- *I have **some** money.*
- *Would you like **some** tea?* (offer)
- *Can I have **some** water?* (request)

## Any

**Negative** sentences and **questions**:
- *I don't have **any** money.*
- *Do you have **any** questions?*
- *Is there **any** milk?*

**Positive** (= it doesn't matter which):
- ***Any** color is fine.*
- *Come **any** time you like.*

## No / None

**No** + noun (determiner):
- *There is **no** milk.*
- *I have **no** idea.*
- *There were **no** problems.*

**None** alone (pronoun):
- *How much is left?* — ***None**.*
- ***None** of them came.*
- *I wanted cake but there was **none**.*

## Compounds

| Some- | Any- | No- |
|-------|------|-----|
| someone | anyone | no one |
| somebody | anybody | nobody |
| something | anything | nothing |
| somewhere | anywhere | nowhere |

- *I saw **someone**.*
- *I didn't see **anyone**.*
- *I saw **no one**.*

## Double Negative Warning

- ✗ *I don't have no money.* (double negative)
- ✓ *I don't have any money.*
- ✓ *I have no money.*
