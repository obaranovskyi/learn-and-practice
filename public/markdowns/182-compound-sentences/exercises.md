# Exercises: Compound Sentences

Join with appropriate punctuation:

1. He studied hard. He passed.
2. It was late. We left.
3. She's talented. She'll succeed.
4. I wanted to go. I was too tired.
5. Hurry up. You'll miss the bus.

**Answers:** 1. He studied hard, so he passed. 2. It was late, so we left. 3. She's talented; therefore, she'll succeed. 4. I wanted to go, but I was too tired. 5. Hurry up, or you'll miss the bus.
