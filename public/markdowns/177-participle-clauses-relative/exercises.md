# Exercises: Participle Clauses (Relative)

Reduce the relative clauses:

1. The woman who is sitting there is my aunt.
2. The letter which was written in French was difficult.
3. People who work here are friendly.
4. The car which was stolen was found.
5. Anyone who wants to help is welcome.

**Answers:** 1. The woman sitting there 2. The letter written in French 3. People working here 4. The car stolen 5. Anyone wanting to help
