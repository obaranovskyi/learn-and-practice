# Exercises: Absolute Constructions

Rewrite using absolute construction: 1. If the weather permits, we'll go. 2. When the work was done, they left. 3. After all things are considered, it's a good plan. 4. Because his task was finished, he relaxed. 5. If God is willing, we'll meet again.

**Answers:** 1. Weather permitting, we'll go. 2. The work done, they left. 3. All things considered, it's a good plan. 4. His task finished, he relaxed. 5. God willing, we'll meet again.
