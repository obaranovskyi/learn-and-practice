# Exercises: Collective Nouns

Choose the best verb (both may be acceptable):

1. The team (is/are) celebrating their victory.
2. The police (is/are) looking for the suspect.
3. My family (live/lives) in different cities.
4. The audience (was/were) applauding.
5. The staff (has/have) received their bonuses.

**Answers:** 1. is (unit) or are (individuals) 2. are 3. live (different cities = individuals) 4. was/were 5. have (their = individuals)
