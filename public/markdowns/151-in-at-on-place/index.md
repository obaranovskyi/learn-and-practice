# In vs At vs On (Place)

## At - Points/Locations

- Specific places: *at the door, at the bus stop*
- Addresses: *at 45 Oak Street*
- Events: *at a party, at a concert*
- General location: *at home, at work, at school*
- Buildings (as location): *at the supermarket*

## On - Surfaces/Lines

- Surfaces: *on the table, on the floor*
- Walls/ceilings: *on the wall*
- Pages: *on page 5*
- Roads/streets: *on Main Street, on the highway*
- Floors: *on the 5th floor*
- Transport: *on the bus, on the train*
- Edges: *on the coast, on the border*

## In - Enclosed Spaces

- Rooms/buildings: *in the kitchen, in the building*
- Cities/countries: *in London, in France*
- Water: *in the water, in the sea*
- Books: *in the book, in the newspaper*
- Pictures: *in the photo, in the mirror*
- Vehicles: *in the car, in a taxi*

## Key Distinctions

| At | In |
|----|----|
| at the office (location) | in the office (inside) |
| at school (activity) | in the school (building) |
| at the corner | in/on the corner |

## Fixed Expressions

- *at home, at work, at school*
- *in bed, in hospital, in prison*
- *on TV, on the radio, on the phone*
