# Prepositions After Nouns

Some nouns are followed by specific prepositions.

## Noun + of

- advantage of, cause of
- example of, lack of
- photo/picture of, possibility of
- result of, risk of

*There's no **lack of** talent here.*

## Noun + for

- need for, reason for
- demand for, desire for
- respect for, responsibility for
- room for, search for

*There's no **need for** worry.*

## Noun + in

- belief in, increase in
- decrease in, interest in
- rise in, fall in

*There's been an **increase in** sales.*

## Noun + to

- answer to, damage to
- invitation to, key to
- reaction to, response to
- solution to, threat to

*What's the **answer to** this question?*

## Noun + on

- effect on, impact on
- influence on, tax on
- ban on, emphasis on

*It has an **effect on** health.*

## Noun + with

- connection with, contact with
- relationship with, trouble with

*I've lost **contact with** her.*

## Noun + between

- connection between, difference between
- relationship between, link between

*What's the **difference between** them?*
