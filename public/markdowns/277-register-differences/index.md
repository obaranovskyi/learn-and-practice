# Register Differences

Register = level of formality appropriate to situation.

## Vocabulary

| Formal | Informal |
|--------|----------|
| purchase | buy |
| commence | start/begin |
| terminate | end/finish |
| require | need |
| assist | help |
| enquire | ask |
| residence | home |
| deceased | dead |
| intoxicated | drunk |
| sufficient | enough |

## Grammar Differences

### Contractions
- Formal: "I am unable to attend."
- Informal: "I can't come."

### Passive voice
- Formal: "The report was completed."
- Informal: "We finished the report."

### Questions
- Formal: "Would you be so kind as to..."
- Informal: "Can you..."

### Phrasal verbs
- Formal: "The meeting was postponed."
- Informal: "The meeting was put off."

## Sentence Length

- Formal: Longer, complex sentences
- Informal: Shorter, simpler sentences

## When to Use

### Formal
- Academic writing
- Business correspondence
- Official documents
- Job interviews
- Presentations

### Informal
- Friends/family
- Text messages
- Casual conversations
- Personal emails
- Social media

## Mixing Registers

Avoid sudden shifts:
- ❌ "The committee convened to figure stuff out."
- ✅ "The committee convened to discuss the issues."
