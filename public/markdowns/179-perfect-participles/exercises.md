# Exercises: Perfect Participles

Rewrite using perfect participles:

1. After I had eaten, I felt better.
2. Because she had seen the movie, she didn't go.
3. After they had been told, they understood.
4. Since he had finished, he could relax.
5. After it had been repaired, it worked.

**Answers:** 1. Having eaten, I felt better. 2. Having seen the movie, she didn't go. 3. Having been told, they understood. 4. Having finished, he could relax. 5. Having been repaired, it worked.
