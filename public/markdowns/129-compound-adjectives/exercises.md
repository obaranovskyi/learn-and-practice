# Exercises: Compound Adjectives

Create compound adjectives:

1. a flight that lasts three hours = a ___ flight
2. a child who is ten years old = a ___ child
3. a person who works hard = a ___ person
4. a meal cooked at home = a ___ meal
5. a decision made at the last minute = a ___ decision

**Answers:** 1. three-hour 2. ten-year-old 3. hard-working 4. home-cooked 5. last-minute
