# Colons

Colons (:) introduce what follows.

## Before Lists
- *I need three things: milk, bread, and eggs.*
- *The colors are: red, blue, and green.*

**Note**: Only after a complete sentence:
- ✅ *I need: milk, eggs.*
- ❌ *I need: milk, eggs.* (if "I need" is incomplete without the list)

## Before Explanations
- *There's one problem: we have no money.*
- *She had one goal: to win the race.*

## Before Quotes
- *He said: "I'll be there."*
- *The sign read: "No Entry".*

## In Time
- *The meeting is at 3:30 PM.*
- *It's 10:15.*

## In Ratios
- *The ratio is 2:1.*
- *Mix water and flour 3:1.*

## In Titles
- *Star Wars: A New Hope*
- *The Book: A Complete Guide*

## After Salutations (formal)
- *Dear Sir:* (formal letter)
- *To Whom It May Concern:*

## NOT for These Uses
- ❌ After "such as" or "including"
- ❌ After "is" or "are" directly before list
  - ❌ *My hobbies are: reading and swimming.*
  - ✅ *My hobbies are reading and swimming.*
