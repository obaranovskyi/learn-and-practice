# Must have / Can't have (Past Deduction)

## Structure: modal + have + past participle

### Must have (almost certain - past)
- She **must have forgotten**. (I'm sure she forgot)
- He **must have been** tired. (I'm sure he was)

### Can't/Couldn't have (impossible - past)
- She **can't have said** that! (impossible!)
- He **couldn't have known**. (impossible)

## Examples
- The ground is wet. It **must have rained**.
- She **can't have finished** already! (impossible)
