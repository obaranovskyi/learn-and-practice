# Contrasting Ideas

Words and phrases that show differences.

## Sentence Connectors

- **However**: *It was expensive. However, I bought it.*
- **Nevertheless/Nonetheless**: *He was tired. Nevertheless, he continued.*
- **On the other hand**: *She's strict. On the other hand, she's fair.*
- **In contrast**: *John is quiet. In contrast, Mary is outgoing.*
- **On the contrary**: (contradicts previous statement) *It's not boring. On the contrary, it's fascinating.*

## Conjunctions

- **But**: *It's old but reliable.*
- **Although/Though/Even though**: *Although it rained, we went out.*
- **While/Whereas**: *She likes coffee, while he prefers tea.*
- **Despite/In spite of** (+ noun/gerund): *Despite the rain, we went out.*

## Adverbs

- **Yet**: *It's simple yet effective.*
- **Still**: *He's young. Still, he's very mature.*

## Concession

Acknowledging opposing point:
- *While I understand your concern, I disagree.*
- *Admittedly, it's expensive, but it's worth it.*
- *It's true that... However...*

## Comparison
| Formal | Informal |
|--------|----------|
| However | But |
| Nevertheless | Still |
| In contrast | On the other hand |
| Although | Though |

## Punctuation
- After sentence connectors: comma
- With conjunctions: no comma before but (short clauses)
