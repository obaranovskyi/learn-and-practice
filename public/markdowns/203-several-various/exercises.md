# Exercises: Several/Various

Fill in several or various:

1. I've asked ___ people, but no one knows.
2. The store sells ___ types of coffee.
3. She called me ___ times yesterday.
4. We tried ___ approaches to solve the problem.
5. ___ students raised their hands.

**Answers:** 1. several 2. various 3. several 4. various 5. Several
