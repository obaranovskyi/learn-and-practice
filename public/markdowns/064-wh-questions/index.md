# Wh- Questions

Form: **Wh-word + Auxiliary + Subject + Verb...?**

## Question Words
- **What** - things
- **Where** - place
- **When** - time
- **Why** - reason
- **Who** - person
- **Which** - choice
- **How** - manner/way
- **Whose** - possession

## Examples
- **What** do you want?
- **Where** does she live?
- **When** did they arrive?
- **Why** are you crying?
- **How** does this work?
