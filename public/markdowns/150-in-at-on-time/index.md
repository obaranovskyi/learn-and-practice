# In vs At vs On (Time)

## At - Specific Times

- Clock times: *at 3 o'clock, at 7:30*
- Mealtimes: *at breakfast, at lunch*
- Specific moments: *at noon, at midnight, at sunset*
- Holiday periods: *at Christmas, at Easter* (BrE)
- *at the moment, at present, at the weekend* (BrE)

## On - Days and Dates

- Days: *on Monday, on Friday*
- Dates: *on July 4th, on the 25th*
- Special days: *on my birthday, on Christmas Day*
- Day + time: *on Monday morning*
- *on the weekend* (AmE)

## In - Longer Periods

- Parts of day: *in the morning, in the afternoon*
- Months: *in June, in December*
- Seasons: *in summer, in winter*
- Years: *in 2024, in 1990*
- Decades/centuries: *in the 1990s, in the 21st century*
- *in time* (early enough)

## No Preposition

- *last/next/this/every*: *last week, next month*
- *yesterday, today, tomorrow*

## Exceptions

- *at night* (not in the night)
- *on time* (punctual) vs *in time* (early enough)
- *in the end* (finally) vs *at the end* (location)
