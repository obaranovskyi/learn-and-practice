# Exercises: Indefinite Pronouns

Fill in the correct indefinite pronoun:

1. Is ___ there? (question)
2. I have ___ to tell you. (positive)
3. ___ called while you were out.
4. There's ___ in the fridge. (negative meaning)
5. ___ knows the answer.

**Answers:** 1. anyone/anybody 2. something 3. Someone/Somebody 4. nothing 5. Everyone/Everybody (or Nobody)
