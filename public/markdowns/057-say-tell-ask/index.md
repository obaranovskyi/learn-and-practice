# Say vs Tell vs Ask

## Say
- No object required
- He **said** (that) he was tired.
- ❌ He said me...

## Tell
- Object required
- He **told me** (that) he was tired.
- ❌ He told that...

## Ask
- For questions: **asked** (if/what...)
- For requests: **asked** + object + to-infinitive

## Common Expressions
- say hello/goodbye, say sorry
- tell a story/joke/lie, tell the truth, tell the difference
