# Shall

## Uses (mainly British, formal)

### 1. Offers
- **Shall** I help you?
- **Shall** I open the window?

### 2. Suggestions (with we)
- **Shall** we go now?
- What **shall** we do?

### 3. Legal/Formal Documents
- The tenant **shall** pay rent monthly.

## Shall vs Will
- In modern English, **will** is more common
- **Shall** is mainly used for offers/suggestions with I/we
