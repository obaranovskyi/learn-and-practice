# Exercises: Not only...but also

Complete the sentences:

1. She's ___ beautiful ___ intelligent.
2. He ___ speaks English ___ French.
3. ___ did he apologize, ___ he also sent flowers.
4. ___ the food ___ the service was excellent.
5. She can ___ sing ___ dance.

**Answers:** 1. not only, but also 2. not only, but also 3. Not only, but 4. Not only, but also 5. not only, but also
