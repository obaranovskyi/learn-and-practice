# Exercises: Of + Quantifier

Add "of" where necessary (or write "no of needed"):

1. Most ___ students passed.
2. Most ___ the students passed.
3. Some ___ them came.
4. All ___ my friends know.
5. Half ___ the cake is gone.

**Answers:** 1. no of needed 2. of 3. of 4. of (optional) 5. of (optional)
