# Exercises: Articles with Uncountable Nouns

Add the correct article (the or Ø):

1. ___ information you gave me was helpful.
2. ___ money can't buy ___ happiness.
3. I need ___ advice about my career.
4. ___ coffee in this cup is too hot.
5. ___ knowledge is power.

**Answers:** 1. The 2. Ø, Ø 3. Ø (or some) 4. The 5. Ø
