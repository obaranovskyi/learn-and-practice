# Reporting Statements

## Tense Backshift

| Direct | Reported |
|--------|----------|
| "I **am** tired" | He said he **was** tired |
| "I **work** here" | She said she **worked** there |
| "I **am working**" | He said he **was working** |
| "I **have finished**" | She said she **had finished** |
| "I **worked**" | He said he **had worked** |
| "I **will come**" | She said she **would come** |
| "I **can help**" | He said he **could help** |

## Structure
Subject + said (that) + reported clause

## No backshift needed when:
- The statement is still true
- Reporting immediately
