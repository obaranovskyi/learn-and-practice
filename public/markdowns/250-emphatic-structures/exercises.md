# Exercises: Emphatic Structures

Rewrite for emphasis on underlined part: 1. _Mary_ called you. 2. I need _help_. 3. He finished it. (use 'did') 4. _The manager_ approved it. 5. I want _peace_.

**Answers:** 1. It was Mary who called you. 2. What I need is help. 3. He did finish it. 4. It was the manager who approved it. 5. All I want is peace.
