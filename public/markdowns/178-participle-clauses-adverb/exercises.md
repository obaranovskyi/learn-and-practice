# Exercises: Participle Clauses (Adverb)

Reduce using participles:

1. Because she was hungry, she ate.
2. While I was walking, I saw him.
3. After he had finished, he left.
4. Since the house was built in 1900, it's old.
5. Because I didn't know the answer, I guessed.

**Answers:** 1. Being hungry, she ate. 2. While walking, I saw him. 3. Having finished, he left. 4. Built in 1900, it's old. 5. Not knowing the answer, I guessed.
