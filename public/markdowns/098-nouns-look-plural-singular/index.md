# Nouns That Look Plural but Are Singular

These nouns end in -s but take singular verbs.

## Academic Subjects

- *Mathematics is difficult.* (also: maths)
- *Physics is interesting.*
- *Economics deals with markets.*
- *Politics is complicated.*
- *Statistics is useful.* (as a subject)
- *Linguistics studies language.*

## Diseases

- *Measles is contagious.*
- *Mumps is common in children.*
- *Rabies is dangerous.*

## Activities/Games

- *Billiards is fun.*
- *Darts is popular in pubs.*
- *Aerobics is good exercise.*
- *Gymnastics requires flexibility.*

## News & Other

- *The news is shocking.*
- *The United States is a large country.*
- *The Netherlands is in Europe.*

## Exception: Statistics

- *Statistics is my favorite subject.* (the field)
- *The statistics are misleading.* (the data/numbers)

## Common Mistakes

- ✓ *The news is good.*
- ✗ *The news are good.*
- ✓ *Mathematics is hard.*
- ✗ *Mathematics are hard.*
