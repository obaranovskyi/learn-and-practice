# Exercises: Nouns That Look Plural

Choose the correct verb:

1. The news (is/are) disappointing.
2. Physics (is/are) my favorite subject.
3. The United States (has/have) 50 states.
4. Mumps (is/are) a childhood disease.
5. Gymnastics (require/requires) dedication.

**Answers:** 1. is 2. is 3. has 4. is 5. requires
