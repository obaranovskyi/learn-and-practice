# Exercises: Adjective vs Adverb

Choose the correct form:

1. She speaks English (good/well).
2. The food tastes (delicious/deliciously).
3. He works (hard/hardly) ever.
4. I've seen him a lot (late/lately).
5. This is a (real/really) problem.

**Answers:** 1. well 2. delicious 3. hardly 4. lately 5. real
