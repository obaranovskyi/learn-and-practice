# Exercises: Countable Quantifiers

Choose the correct quantifier:

1. (Many/Much) students attended the lecture.
2. I have (few/little) friends, but they're close.
3. (Several/Much) people called while you were out.
4. How (many/much) questions are there?
5. (A number of/An amount of) complaints were filed.

**Answers:** 1. Many 2. few 3. Several 4. many 5. A number of
