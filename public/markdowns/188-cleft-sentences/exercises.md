# Exercises: Cleft Sentences

Rewrite as cleft sentences, emphasizing the underlined part:

1. **John** broke the window.
2. I need **a vacation**.
3. She called **yesterday**.
4. I want **peace**.
5. **The noise** bothered me.

**Answers:** 1. It was John who broke the window. 2. What I need is a vacation. 3. It was yesterday that she called. 4. What I want is peace / All I want is peace. 5. It was the noise that bothered me.
