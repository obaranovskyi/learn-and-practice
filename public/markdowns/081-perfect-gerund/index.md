# Perfect Gerund

## Form: having + past participle

## Use: Action completed before main verb

## Examples
- He denied **having stolen** the money.
- I regret **having said** that.
- She admitted **having made** a mistake.

## Compare
- I remember **doing** it. (simple - general)
- I remember **having done** it. (emphasizes completion)

## Common with
- admit, deny, regret, remember, recall, mention

## Also after prepositions
- **After having finished**, he left.
- **Without having eaten**, she went to bed.
