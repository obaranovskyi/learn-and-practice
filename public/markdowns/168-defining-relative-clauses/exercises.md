# Exercises: Defining Relative Clauses

Combine the sentences:

1. I met a man. He works at your company.
2. She bought a dress. It was on sale.
3. That's the restaurant. We ate there.
4. I know the reason. He left for that reason.
5. The book is mine. It's on the table.

**Answers:** 1. I met a man who works at your company. 2. She bought a dress that was on sale. 3. That's the restaurant where we ate. 4. I know the reason why he left. 5. The book that/which is on the table is mine.
