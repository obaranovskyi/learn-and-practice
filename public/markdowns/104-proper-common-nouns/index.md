# Proper vs Common Nouns

## Common Nouns

General names for people, places, things - **lowercase**:
- *city, country, river, mountain*
- *book, car, phone, computer*
- *teacher, doctor, student*
- *day, month, language*

## Proper Nouns

Specific names - **always capitalized**:
- *London, France, Amazon, Everest*
- *Harry Potter, Toyota, iPhone*
- *Dr. Smith, President Biden*
- *Monday, January, English*

## Capitalization Rules

**Capitalize**:
- First word of sentence
- Names of people, places
- Days, months, holidays
- Titles before names: *President Lincoln*
- Languages, nationalities
- Brand names

**Don't capitalize**:
- Seasons: *spring, summer*
- Directions (general): *go north*
- Job titles (general): *He's a president.*
