# Exercises: Proper vs Common

Capitalize where necessary:

1. we visited paris in june.
2. she speaks french and spanish.
3. the doctor works at st. mary's hospital.
4. i read the great gatsby last summer.
5. they celebrate christmas every december.

**Answers:** 1. We, Paris, June 2. She, French, Spanish 3. The, St. Mary's Hospital 4. I, The Great Gatsby 5. They, Christmas, December
