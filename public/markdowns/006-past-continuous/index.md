# Past Continuous

Actions in progress at a specific past time. Form: **was/were + verb-ing**

## Uses

### 1. Action in Progress at a Past Time
- At 8 PM, I **was watching** TV.

### 2. Background Action (interrupted)
- I **was cooking** when the phone rang.
- While she **was sleeping**, it started to rain.

### 3. Parallel Actions
- While I **was reading**, he **was watching** TV.

### 4. Setting the Scene
- The sun **was shining** and birds **were singing**.

## Past Continuous vs Simple Past

| Past Continuous | Simple Past |
|-----------------|-------------|
| Longer action (background) | Shorter action (interruption) |
| I was cooking... | ...when the phone rang. |
