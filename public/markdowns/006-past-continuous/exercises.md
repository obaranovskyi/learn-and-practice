# Exercises: Past Continuous

## Exercise 1: Complete

1. I ______ (sleep) when you called.
2. What ______ you ______ (do) at 9 PM?
3. While she ______ (cook), he ______ (watch) TV.

**Answers:** 1. was sleeping 2. were, doing 3. was cooking, was watching

---

## Exercise 2: Past Simple or Continuous?

1. I ______ (read) when the lights ______ (go) out.
2. She ______ (walk) home when she ______ (see) an accident.

**Answers:** 1. was reading, went 2. was walking, saw
