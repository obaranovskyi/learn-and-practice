# Time Sequencing & Consistency

## Tense Consistency
Keep the same time frame unless time changes.

### In Past Narratives
✅ He **arrived**, **sat** down, and **ordered** a coffee.
❌ He arrived, sits down, and ordered.

## Sequencing Actions
- **First**, I woke up. **Then**, I had breakfast. **After that**, I left.
- **Before** leaving, I checked my bag.
- **While** cooking, I listened to music.

## Time Markers
- Sequential: first, then, next, after that, finally
- Simultaneous: while, as, during
- Prior: before, previously
