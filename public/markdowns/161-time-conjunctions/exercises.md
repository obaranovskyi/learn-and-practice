# Exercises: Time Conjunctions

Fill in and use correct tense:

1. I'll call you ___ I (arrive).
2. ___ you finish, let me know.
3. She was reading ___ I was cooking.
4. Wait here ___ I come back.
5. ___ I see her, I think of you.

**Answers:** 1. when/as soon as, arrive 2. When/Once 3. while 4. until 5. Whenever
