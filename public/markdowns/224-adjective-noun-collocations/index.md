# Adjective + Noun Collocations

Certain adjectives naturally pair with certain nouns.

## Strong/Weak
- **strong** coffee/tea (NOT powerful coffee)
- **strong** wind/accent
- **weak** tea (NOT light tea in this context)

## Heavy/Light
- **heavy** rain/traffic/smoker
- **light** rain/traffic/sleeper

## Big/Great
- **big** mistake/problem/deal
- **great** success/importance

## Deep/High/Low
- **deep** breath/sleep/trouble
- **high** speed/price/quality
- **low** price/quality

## Common Pairs
- fast food, quick shower
- broad/wide range
- narrow escape
- bright future/idea
- sharp rise/fall
- golden opportunity
- serious problem/injury
- close friend/relationship
- exact/precise moment
- main reason/point

## Common Mistakes
- ❌ powerful coffee → ✅ strong coffee
- ❌ big rain → ✅ heavy rain
- ❌ fast food (NOT quick food)
