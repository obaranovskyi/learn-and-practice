# Exercises: Coordinating Conjunctions

Fill in and, but, or, so, yet:

1. She's tired, ___ she keeps working.
2. Do you want coffee ___ tea?
3. He studied hard, ___ he passed.
4. It's expensive ___ beautiful.
5. I was hungry, ___ I made a sandwich.

**Answers:** 1. yet/but 2. or 3. so 4. but/and 5. so
