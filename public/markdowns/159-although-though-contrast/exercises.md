# Exercises: Contrast Conjunctions

Fill in although, though, even though, or despite:

1. ___ being tired, she went to work.
2. ___ it was expensive, I bought it.
3. He's nice. He talks too much, ___.
4. ___ I explained it twice, she didn't understand.
5. ___ he's rich, he lives simply.

**Answers:** 1. Despite 2. Although/Though 3. though 4. Even though 5. Although/Though
