# Exercises: Conditional Conjunctions

Fill in if, unless, or in case:

1. I'll tell her ___ I see her.
2. Take your phone ___ you need to call.
3. ___ you study, you won't pass.
4. I won't go ___ you come with me.
5. Write it down ___ you forget.

**Answers:** 1. if 2. in case 3. Unless 4. unless 5. in case
