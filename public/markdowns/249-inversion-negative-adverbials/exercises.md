# Exercises: Inversion

Rewrite with inversion: 1. I have never seen such a thing. 2. She rarely complains. 3. They had no sooner arrived than it rained. 4. He little knew the truth. 5. We only then understood.

**Answers:** 1. Never have I seen... 2. Rarely does she complain. 3. No sooner had they arrived than... 4. Little did he know... 5. Only then did we understand.
