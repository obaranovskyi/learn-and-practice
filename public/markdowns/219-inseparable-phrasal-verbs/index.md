# Inseparable Phrasal Verbs

Inseparable phrasal verbs cannot have an object between verb and particle.

## Structure

- ✅ look after the children
- ❌ look the children after
- ✅ look after them
- ❌ look them after

## Common Inseparable Phrasal Verbs

### With Objects
- **look after**: care for → *Look after the baby.*
- **look for**: search → *I'm looking for my keys.*
- **look into**: investigate → *Police looked into it.*
- **get on/off**: enter/exit vehicle → *Get on the bus.*
- **get over**: recover from → *She got over the illness.*
- **run into**: meet by chance → *I ran into an old friend.*
- **go through**: experience → *He went through hard times.*
- **come across**: find accidentally → *I came across this photo.*

### Without Objects (Intransitive)
- **break down**: stop working → *The car broke down.*
- **come back**: return → *When will you come back?*
- **grow up**: become adult → *I grew up in London.*
- **wake up**: stop sleeping → *I wake up at 7.*
