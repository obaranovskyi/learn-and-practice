# It's time / It's high time

## It's time + to-infinitive (general)
- **It's time to go**.
- **It's time to eat**.

## It's time + past simple (criticism/urgency)
- **It's time** you **went** to bed. (you should go now)
- **It's time** we **left**. (we should leave now)

## It's high time / It's about time (stronger)
- **It's high time** you **got** a job!
- **It's about time** she **learned** to drive.

## Note
Past tense is used but meaning is present/future.
