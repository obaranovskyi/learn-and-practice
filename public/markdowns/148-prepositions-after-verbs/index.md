# Prepositions After Verbs

Many verbs require specific prepositions.

## Verb + for

- apologize for, apply for, ask for
- blame for, look for, pay for
- search for, wait for, thank for

*She **apologized for** being late.*

## Verb + to

- belong to, listen to, talk to
- speak to, refer to, reply to
- happen to, object to, look forward to

*I **listen to** music daily.*

## Verb + about

- care about, complain about
- dream about, forget about
- talk about, think about, worry about

*Don't **worry about** it.*

## Verb + on

- concentrate on, depend on
- insist on, rely on, spend on

*It **depends on** the weather.*

## Verb + in

- believe in, participate in
- succeed in, specialize in

*I **believe in** you.*

## Verb + with

- agree with, argue with
- deal with, help with, provide with

*I **agree with** you.*

## Verb + of

- approve of, consist of
- dream of, think of, remind of

*It **consists of** three parts.*

## Verb + from

- borrow from, differ from
- prevent from, suffer from

*This **differs from** that.*
