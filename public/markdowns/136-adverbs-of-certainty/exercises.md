# Exercises: Adverbs of Certainty

Choose the best adverb:

1. It's cloudy. It will ___ rain. (75%)
2. I'm 100% sure. She will ___ be there.
3. I'm not sure. ___ I'll come. (50%)
4. It's clear from the evidence. He's ___ guilty.
5. There's a small chance. They ___ won't agree.

**Answers:** 1. probably 2. definitely/certainly 3. Maybe/Perhaps 4. obviously/clearly 5. probably
