# Exercises: Demonstrative Pronouns

Fill in this, that, these, or those:

1. What's ___ in your hand?
2. ___ over there is my house.
3. ___ were the best years of my life.
4. I'll take ___. (pointing to something far)
5. Hello, ___ is Sarah speaking.

**Answers:** 1. this 2. That 3. Those 4. those 5. this
