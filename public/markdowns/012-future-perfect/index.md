# Future Perfect Simple

Actions completed before a future point. Form: **will have + past participle**

## Uses

### Before a Future Deadline
- By next month, I **will have finished** the project.
- By 2030, she **will have graduated**.

### Time Expressions
by tomorrow, by next week, by the time, before

## Example
- By 6 PM, I **will have cooked** dinner.
(action complete before 6 PM)
