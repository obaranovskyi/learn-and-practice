# Exercises: Double Comparatives

Complete the sentences:

1. The ___ you practice, the ___ you'll become.
2. The weather is getting ___ and ___.
3. ___ harder you study, ___ better your grades.
4. The ___, the ___ (short = good).
5. Life is becoming more and ___ complicated.

**Answers:** 1. more, better 2. colder/colder (or worse/worse) 3. The, the 4. sooner, better 5. more
