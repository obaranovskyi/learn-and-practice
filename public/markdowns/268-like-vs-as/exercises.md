# Exercises: Like vs As

Choose like or as: 1. She works ___ a doctor. 2. He looks ___ his father. 3. ___ I mentioned, we're late. 4. Do it ___ I showed you. 5. It smells ___ roses.

**Answers:** 1. as (function) 2. like (resemblance) 3. As (before clause) 4. as (before clause) 5. like (before noun)
