# Must / Have to / Have got to

## Obligation

### Must (personal/internal)
- I **must** finish this today. (I feel it's important)
- You **must** try this cake! (strong recommendation)

### Have to (external)
- I **have to** wear a uniform. (rule)
- She **has to** work on Saturdays. (job requirement)

### Have got to (informal = have to)
- I**'ve got to** go now.

## Negative

| Positive | Negative |
|----------|----------|
| must (obligation) | must not (prohibition) |
| have to (obligation) | don't have to (no obligation) |

- You **mustn't** smoke here. (prohibited)
- You **don't have to** come. (not necessary)

## Past
- I **had to** work late yesterday.
- (No past form of "must" for obligation)
