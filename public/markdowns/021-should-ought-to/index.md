# Should / Ought to

## Meaning (same meaning, slightly different usage)
- **Should** = more common
- **Ought to** = slightly more formal

## Uses

### Advice
- You **should** see a doctor.
- You **ought to** exercise more.

### Expectation
- The train **should** arrive soon.
- She **ought to** be here by now.

### Mild Obligation
- Students **should** do their homework.

## Negative
- You **shouldn't** smoke.
- You **ought not to** (rare, formal)
