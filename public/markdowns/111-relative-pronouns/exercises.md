# Exercises: Relative Pronouns

Fill in who, whom, whose, which, or that:

1. The student ___ won the prize is my friend.
2. The book ___ I'm reading is fascinating.
3. The woman ___ car was stolen called the police.
4. Is this the movie ___ you recommended?
5. The man to ___ I spoke was helpful.

**Answers:** 1. who/that 2. which/that 3. whose 4. that/which 5. whom
