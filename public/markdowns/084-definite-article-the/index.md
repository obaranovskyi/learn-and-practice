# Definite Article "the"

The definite article **"the"** is used when referring to something specific or already known.

## When to Use "the"

1. **Previously mentioned**: *I saw a cat. The cat was black.*
2. **Unique things**: *the sun, the moon, the sky*
3. **Superlatives**: *the best, the tallest, the most beautiful*
4. **Ordinals**: *the first, the second, the last*
5. **Only one exists**: *the President, the capital*
6. **Both speaker and listener know**: *Can you close the door?*
7. **With of-phrases**: *the center of the city*
8. **Musical instruments**: *play the piano, the guitar*
9. **Inventions/species**: *The telephone changed communication.*

## Pronunciation

- Before consonants: /ðə/ - *the book*
- Before vowels: /ði/ - *the apple*

## Examples

- *The movie we watched was excellent.*
- *She's the best student in class.*
- *The Nile is the longest river in Africa.*
