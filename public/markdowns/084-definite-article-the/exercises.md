# Exercises: Definite Article "the"

Add "the" where necessary:

1. ___ sun rises in ___ east.
2. She plays ___ violin beautifully.
3. ___ Amazon is ___ largest rainforest.
4. Can you pass me ___ salt?
5. He's ___ tallest person in ___ room.

**Answers:** 1. The, the 2. the 3. The, the 4. the 5. the, the
