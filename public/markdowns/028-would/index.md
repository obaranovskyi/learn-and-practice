# Would

## Uses

### 1. Polite Requests
- **Would** you help me?
- **Would** you mind closing the door?

### 2. Hypothetical/Imaginary
- I **would** buy it if I had money.
- What **would** you do?

### 3. Past Habits (formal)
- He **would** always arrive late. (= used to)
- We **would** play here as children.

### 4. Preferences
- I**'d** rather stay home.
- **Would** you like some tea?

### 5. Refusal in Past (wouldn't)
- The car **wouldn't** start.
- She **wouldn't** listen.
