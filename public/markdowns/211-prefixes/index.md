# Prefixes
Prefixes are added to the beginning of words to change meaning.
## Common Negative Prefixes
- **un-**: unhappy, unable, unfair
- **dis-**: disagree, disappear, disconnect
- **in-/im-/il-/ir-**: impossible, illegal, irregular
- **non-**: non-stop, non-fiction
## Other Prefixes
- **re-**: redo, rewrite (again)
- **pre-**: preview, prehistoric (before)
- **post-**: postwar, postpone (after)
- **mis-**: misunderstand, misbehave (wrong)
- **over-**: overwork, overcook (too much)
- **under-**: underpay, underestimate (too little)
- **anti-**: antisocial, antiwar (against)
- **super-**: superhuman, superstar (above)
