# Exercises: Simple Present

## Exercise 1: Fill in the Blanks
Complete the sentences with the correct form of the verb in brackets.

1. She ______ (work) in a hospital.
2. They ______ (not/like) spicy food.
3. ______ he ______ (speak) French?
4. The train ______ (leave) at 6 PM.
5. My brother ______ (study) medicine.

**Answers:**
1. works
2. don't like
3. Does, speak
4. leaves
5. studies

---

## Exercise 2: Correct the Mistakes
Find and correct the errors in these sentences.

1. He don't understand the question.
2. She watchs TV every evening.
3. Do she like chocolate?
4. The children plays in the park.
5. My father work in an office.

**Answers:**
1. He **doesn't** understand the question.
2. She **watches** TV every evening.
3. **Does** she like chocolate?
4. The children **play** in the park.
5. My father **works** in an office.

---

## Exercise 3: Make Questions
Turn these statements into questions.

1. She speaks Spanish.
2. They live in Paris.
3. He plays football.
4. You work here.
5. The bus arrives at noon.

**Answers:**
1. Does she speak Spanish?
2. Do they live in Paris?
3. Does he play football?
4. Do you work here?
5. Does the bus arrive at noon?

---

## Exercise 4: Negative Sentences
Make these sentences negative.

1. I like horror movies.
2. She eats meat.
3. We go to school on Saturdays.
4. He knows the answer.
5. They live near the station.

**Answers:**
1. I don't like horror movies.
2. She doesn't eat meat.
3. We don't go to school on Saturdays.
4. He doesn't know the answer.
5. They don't live near the station.

---

## Exercise 5: Choose the Correct Option

1. Water (boil / boils) at 100 degrees Celsius.
2. My parents (doesn't / don't) speak English.
3. (Do / Does) your sister work here?
4. The shop (open / opens) at 9 AM.
5. I usually (wake / wakes) up early.

**Answers:**
1. boils
2. don't
3. Does
4. opens
5. wake
