# Exercises: Complex Sentences

Identify the dependent clause:

1. When I arrived, she left.
2. I like the book that you gave me.
3. Because it rained, we stayed home.
4. I know what you mean.
5. Although he's young, he's wise.

**Answers:** 1. When I arrived 2. that you gave me 3. Because it rained 4. what you mean 5. Although he's young
