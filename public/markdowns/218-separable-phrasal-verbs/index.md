# Separable Phrasal Verbs

With separable phrasal verbs, the object can go between the verb and particle.

## Structure

- **turn on** the light / turn **the light** on
- **pick up** the book / pick **the book** up
- **put away** your toys / put **your toys** away

## Rule with Pronouns

With pronouns, the object MUST go in the middle:
- ✅ Turn **it** on
- ❌ Turn on it
- ✅ Pick **them** up
- ❌ Pick up them

## Common Separable Phrasal Verbs

- **turn on/off**: Turn the TV on.
- **pick up**: Pick up the kids.
- **put on/away/down**: Put your coat on.
- **take off**: Take your shoes off.
- **throw away**: Throw it away.
- **fill in/out**: Fill the form in.
- **work out**: Work the problem out.
- **figure out**: I can't figure it out.
- **give back**: Give me back my book.
- **bring up**: Don't bring it up.
