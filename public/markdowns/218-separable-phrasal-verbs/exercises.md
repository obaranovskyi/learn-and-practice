# Exercises: Separable Phrasal Verbs

Rewrite with pronoun: 1. Turn on the light. → Turn ___ on. 2. Pick up the boxes. → Pick ___ up. 3. Put away your phone. → Put ___ away. 4. Figure out the answer. → Figure ___ out. 5. Throw away the trash. → Throw ___ away.

**Answers:** 1. it 2. them 3. it 4. it 5. it
