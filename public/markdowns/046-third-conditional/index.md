# Third Conditional

Form: **If + past perfect, would have + past participle**

## Use: Unreal past situations (didn't happen)

### Examples
- If I **had studied**, I **would have passed**. (I didn't study, so I failed)
- If she **had come**, she **would have enjoyed** it.
- I **wouldn't have been** late if I **had left** earlier.

## Expresses
- Regret about the past
- Imagining different past outcomes
