# Past Perfect Simple

The "past of the past" - actions completed before another past action. Form: **had + past participle**

## Uses

### 1. Earlier Past Action
- When I arrived, she **had already left**.
- I **had finished** work before he called.

### 2. Reported Speech
- She said she **had seen** the movie.

### 3. Third Conditional
- If I **had known**, I would have helped.

## Timeline
Past Perfect → Simple Past → Now
(had left) → (arrived) → (now)

## Common Mistakes
❌ When I arrived, she already left.
✅ When I arrived, she **had already** left.
