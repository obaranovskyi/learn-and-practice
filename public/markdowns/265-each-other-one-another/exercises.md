# Exercises: Each Other vs One Another

Both are acceptable. Choose the traditionally "correct" one: 1. The two sisters helped ___. 2. All the team members supported ___. 3. The couple looked at ___. 4. The five students taught ___. 5. We respect ___ opinions.

**Answers:** 1. each other (2 people) 2. one another (more than 2) 3. each other (2 people) 4. one another (more than 2) 5. each other's/one another's (both OK)
