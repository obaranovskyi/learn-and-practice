# Present Continuous Tense

The Present Continuous (also called Present Progressive) describes actions happening now or around the current time.

## Form

**Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing**

### Affirmative
| Subject | Be | Verb-ing |
|---------|-----|----------|
| I | am | working |
| He / She / It | is | working |
| You / We / They | are | working |

### Negative
| Subject | Be + not | Verb-ing |
|---------|----------|----------|
| I | am not (I'm not) | working |
| He / She / It | is not (isn't) | working |
| You / We / They | are not (aren't) | working |

### Question
| Be | Subject | Verb-ing |
|----|---------|----------|
| Am | I | working? |
| Is | he/she/it | working? |
| Are | you/we/they | working? |

## Uses

### 1. Actions Happening Now
Things happening at the moment of speaking.

- I **am reading** a book right now.
- She **is talking** on the phone.
- They **are watching** TV.

### 2. Temporary Situations
Actions happening around the present time but not necessarily at this moment.

- I **am studying** English this semester.
- He **is working** on a new project.
- We **are staying** with friends this week.

### 3. Changing/Developing Situations
- The weather **is getting** warmer.
- Prices **are rising** every year.
- My English **is improving**.

### 4. Future Arrangements
Plans and arrangements already made.

- I **am meeting** John tomorrow.
- We **are flying** to Paris next week.
- She **is starting** a new job on Monday.

### 5. Annoying Habits (with "always")
Used to express irritation about repeated actions.

- He **is always complaining**!
- You **are always losing** your keys!
- She **is constantly interrupting** me!

## Spelling Rules for -ing

| Verb ending | Rule | Example |
|-------------|------|---------|
| Most verbs | Add **-ing** | work → working |
| -e | Drop e, add **-ing** | make → making |
| -ie | Change to **-ying** | lie → lying |
| Short vowel + consonant | Double consonant + **-ing** | run → running |

## Stative Verbs (NOT used in continuous)

Some verbs describe states, not actions, and are NOT normally used in continuous form:

| Category | Verbs |
|----------|-------|
| Emotions | like, love, hate, want, prefer |
| Mental states | know, believe, understand, remember |
| Senses | see, hear, smell, taste |
| Possession | have, own, belong, possess |
| Other | be, seem, appear, mean, need |

❌ I am knowing the answer.  
✅ I **know** the answer.

❌ She is wanting coffee.  
✅ She **wants** coffee.

## Time Expressions

- now, right now, at the moment
- currently, presently
- today, this week/month/year
- still

## Common Mistakes

❌ She working now.  
✅ She **is** working now.

❌ I am work at home today.  
✅ I am **working** at home today.

❌ What you are doing?  
✅ What **are you** doing?
