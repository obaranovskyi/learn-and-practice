# Present Continuous

Describes actions happening now or around the current time.

## Form: Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing

| Subject | Form | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| I | am + verb-ing | I am working |
| He/She/It | is + verb-ing | She is working |
| You/We/They | are + verb-ing | They are working |

## Uses

### 1. Actions Happening Now
- I **am reading** a book right now.
- She **is talking** on the phone.

### 2. Temporary Situations
- I **am staying** with friends this week.
- He **is working** on a new project.

### 3. Future Arrangements
- I **am meeting** John tomorrow.
- We **are flying** to Paris next week.

### 4. Annoying Habits (with "always")
- He **is always complaining**!

## Spelling Rules for -ing

| Ending | Rule | Example |
|--------|------|---------|
| Most verbs | +ing | work → working |
| -e | drop e, +ing | make → making |
| short vowel + consonant | double + ing | run → running |

## Stative Verbs (NOT used in continuous)
know, believe, want, love, hate, own, need

❌ I am knowing → ✅ I **know**
