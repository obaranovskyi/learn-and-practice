# Exercises: Present Continuous

## Exercise 1: Complete the sentences

1. Look! It ______ (rain).
2. She ______ (not/watch) TV now.
3. ______ you ______ (work)?
4. They ______ (play) in the garden.

**Answers:** 1. is raining 2. isn't watching 3. Are, working 4. are playing

---

## Exercise 2: Correct the mistakes

1. He working now.
2. I am not believing you.
3. What she is doing?

**Answers:** 1. He is working 2. I don't believe (stative) 3. What is she doing?

---

## Exercise 3: Write the -ing form

1. make → ______
2. run → ______
3. lie → ______

**Answers:** 1. making 2. running 3. lying
