# Exercises: Present Continuous

## Exercise 1: Fill in the Blanks
Complete with the Present Continuous form.

1. Look! It ______ (rain) outside.
2. They ______ (not/watch) TV right now.
3. ______ you ______ (work) on the project?
4. She ______ (study) for her exams this week.
5. The children ______ (play) in the garden.

**Answers:**
1. is raining
2. aren't watching / are not watching
3. Are, working
4. is studying
5. are playing

---

## Exercise 2: Correct the Mistakes

1. He working at the moment.
2. I am not believing you.
3. What she is doing?
4. They are have lunch now.
5. The baby crying.

**Answers:**
1. He **is** working at the moment.
2. I **don't believe** you. (believe is stative)
3. What **is she** doing?
4. They are **having** lunch now.
5. The baby **is** crying.

---

## Exercise 3: Simple Present or Present Continuous?

1. She usually ______ (drink) tea, but today she ______ (drink) coffee.
2. I ______ (not/understand) this question.
3. Look! The sun ______ (shine).
4. Water ______ (boil) at 100°C.
5. He ______ (work) late this week because of the deadline.

**Answers:**
1. drinks, is drinking
2. don't understand
3. is shining
4. boils
5. is working

---

## Exercise 4: Write the -ing Form

1. make → ______
2. run → ______
3. lie → ______
4. write → ______
5. stop → ______

**Answers:**
1. making
2. running
3. lying
4. writing
5. stopping

## Exercise 5: Make Sentences

Write sentences using Present Continuous for:

1. A temporary situation (you/live with friends)
2. An action happening now (she/talk on the phone)
3. A future arrangement (we/meet tomorrow)
4. An annoying habit (he/always/complain)

**Possible Answers:**
1. I am living with friends this month.
2. She is talking on the phone right now.
3. We are meeting tomorrow at 5 PM.
4. He is always complaining about everything!
