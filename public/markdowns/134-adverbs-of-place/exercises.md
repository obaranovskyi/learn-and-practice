# Exercises: Adverbs of Place

Fill in here, there, somewhere, anywhere, or nowhere:

1. Come ___, please! (to speaker)
2. Is ___ to sit? (question)
3. I left my keys ___. (unknown place)
4. Look over ___! (away from speaker)
5. There's ___ to go in this town. (no place)

**Answers:** 1. here 2. anywhere 3. somewhere 4. there 5. nowhere
