# Adverbs of Place

Adverbs of place tell us **where** something happens.

## Common Adverbs of Place

### Location
- *here, there, everywhere*
- *somewhere, anywhere, nowhere*
- *inside, outside, upstairs, downstairs*
- *nearby, abroad, overseas*

### Direction
- *up, down, in, out*
- *away, back, forward, backward*
- *north, south, east, west*

### Relative Position
- *above, below, behind, ahead*
- *near, far, close by*

## Position in Sentence

Usually at the **end**:
- *Come **here**.*
- *The cat is **outside**.*
- *I looked **everywhere**.*

## Here vs There

- **here** = near the speaker
- **there** = away from the speaker

*Put it **here**. / Put it **there**.*

## Somewhere/Anywhere/Nowhere

- **somewhere** = positive/unknown location
- **anywhere** = questions/negatives/any place
- **nowhere** = no place

*I want to go **somewhere** nice.*
*Is there **anywhere** to eat?*
*There's **nowhere** to hide.*
