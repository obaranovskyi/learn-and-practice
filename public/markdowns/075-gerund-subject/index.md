# Gerund as Subject

## Form: -ing verb as subject

**Swimming** is good exercise.
**Reading** helps you learn.
**Learning** languages takes time.

## Common patterns
- **Verb-ing** is + adjective
- **Verb-ing** makes me + adjective/noun

## Examples
- **Smoking** is bad for your health.
- **Traveling** broadens the mind.
- **Getting** up early is difficult.
- **Living** abroad can be challenging.
