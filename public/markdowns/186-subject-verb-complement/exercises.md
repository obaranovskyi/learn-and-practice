# Exercises: Subject-Verb-Complement

Identify as Object (O) or Complement (C):

1. She is a teacher. ___
2. I love music. ___
3. They made her happy. ___
4. The soup tastes delicious. ___
5. We elected him president. ___

**Answers:** 1. C (subject complement) 2. O 3. C (object complement) 4. C (subject complement) 5. C (object complement)
