# Exercises: Both...and

Complete with both...and:

1. She speaks ___ English ___ Spanish.
2. The movie was ___ funny ___ touching.
3. ___ my brother ___ I play tennis.
4. He's good at ___ math ___ science.
5. She ___ sings ___ plays the piano.

**Answers:** 1. both, and 2. both, and 3. Both, and 4. both, and 5. both, and
