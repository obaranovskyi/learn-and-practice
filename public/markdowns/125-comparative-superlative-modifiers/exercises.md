# Exercises: Comparison Modifiers

Choose the correct modifier:

1. This is (very/much) better than before.
2. She's (a bit/very) taller than me.
3. He's (by far/very) the best player.
4. It's (slightly/very) more expensive.
5. The result was (much/very) worse than expected.

**Answers:** 1. much 2. a bit 3. by far 4. slightly 5. much
