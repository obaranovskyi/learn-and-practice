# Exercises: Omitting Relative Pronouns

Can the relative pronoun be omitted? (Yes/No)

1. The film (that) we watched was good.
2. The woman who lives next door is friendly.
3. My sister, who is a nurse, works nights.
4. The hotel (that) they recommended was full.
5. The person who called left a message.

**Answers:** 1. Yes (object) 2. No (subject) 3. No (non-defining) 4. Yes (object) 5. No (subject)
