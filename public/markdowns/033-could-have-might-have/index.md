# Could have / Might have / May have

## Past Possibility

### May/Might have (something possibly happened)
- She **may have missed** the bus. (possible)
- He **might have forgotten**. (possible)

### Could have (past ability not used / possibility)
- I **could have helped** (but I didn't)
- She **could have gone** anywhere. (possible)

## Different Meanings

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| may/might have | past possibility |
| could have | unused past ability OR possibility |

## Examples
- Where is she? She **might have got** lost.
- I **could have won** but I didn't try.
