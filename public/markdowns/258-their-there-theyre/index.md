# Their vs There vs They're

Three words that sound identical but have different meanings.

## They're = They are

Contraction:
- ***They're** coming to the party.* = They are coming
- ***They're** my friends.* = They are my friends
- ***They're** happy.* = They are happy

## Their = Possession

Belonging to "them":
- ***Their** house is big.*
- *I met **their** parents.*
- ***Their** car broke down.*

## There = Place / Existence

### Location
- *The book is over **there**.*
- *I've been **there** before.*

### Existence (there is/are)
- ***There** is a problem.*
- ***There** are many options.*

## Memory Tricks

- **They're** = they are (apostrophe = missing letter)
- **Their** = possession (has "heir" = inheriting)
- **There** = place (has "here" in it)

## Quick Test

1. Replace with "they are"? → **they're**
2. Shows possession? → **their**
3. Shows place/existence? → **there**

## Examples

| Sentence | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| ___ going home | They're |
| ___ car is red | Their |
| Put it over ___ | there |
| ___ are problems | There |

## Common Mistakes

- ❌ *Their coming soon.*
- ✅ *They're coming soon.*
- ❌ *There dog is cute.*
- ✅ *Their dog is cute.*
