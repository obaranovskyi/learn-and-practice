# Exercises: Quantity Expressions

Choose the correct verb:

1. Most of the cake (is/are) gone.
2. A lot of students (is/are) absent.
3. Ten dollars (is/are) not enough.
4. The number of accidents (is/are) rising.
5. Half of the cookies (is/are) chocolate.

**Answers:** 1. is 2. are 3. is 4. is 5. are
