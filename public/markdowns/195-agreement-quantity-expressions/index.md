# Agreement with Expressions of Quantity

Verb agreement depends on what follows "of."

## "Of" Expressions

### Depends on Noun After "Of"

- *Some **of the water is** dirty.* (uncountable → singular)
- *Some **of the books are** missing.* (countable plural → plural)
- *Most **of the work is** done.* (uncountable → singular)
- *Most **of the students are** here.* (countable plural → plural)

### Applies To
- *all of, some of, most of, none of*
- *half of, part of, a lot of, plenty of*
- *percent/percentage of*
- *a majority/minority of*

## Specific Numbers

### Singular (unit of measure)
- ***Ten miles is** a long way.*
- ***Five dollars is** enough.*
- ***Two weeks is** too long.*

### Plural (individual items)
- ***Ten students are** absent.*
- ***Five books are** on the table.*

## Fractions

- *Half of the pizza **is** gone.*
- *Half of the students **are** here.*
- *Two-thirds of the work **is** done.*
- *Two-thirds of the workers **are** satisfied.*

## "The number of" vs "A number of"

- ***The number of** students **is** increasing.* (singular)
- ***A number of** students **are** absent.* (plural)
