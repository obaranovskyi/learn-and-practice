# Past Perfect Continuous

Duration of activity before another past event. Form: **had been + verb-ing**

## Uses

### Duration Before Past Event
- I **had been waiting** for 2 hours when she arrived.
- He was tired because he **had been working** all day.

### Visible Results in the Past
- Her eyes were red. She **had been crying**.

## vs Past Perfect Simple
- Simple: focus on completion
- Continuous: focus on duration
