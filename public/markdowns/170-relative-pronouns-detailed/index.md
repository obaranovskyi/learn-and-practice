# Relative Pronouns (Detailed)

## Who

**For people** (subject):
- *The woman **who** called is here.*
- *Students **who** study hard succeed.*

## Whom

**For people** (object) - formal:
- *The person **whom** I met was kind.*
- *The candidate **whom** they chose is qualified.*

In informal English, often replaced by "who" or omitted:
- *The person I met...* (no pronoun)

## Which

**For things/animals**:
- *The book **which** I read was good.*
- *The cat **which** lives here is friendly.*

## That

**For people or things** (defining clauses only):
- *The man **that** called is here.*
- *The book **that** I bought is great.*

Preferred after:
- Superlatives: *the best **that** I've seen*
- All/any/every/no/nothing: *everything **that** matters*

## Whose

**For possession** (people or things):
- *The man **whose** car was stolen...*
- *The house **whose** roof is red...*
- *A country **whose** economy is growing...*
