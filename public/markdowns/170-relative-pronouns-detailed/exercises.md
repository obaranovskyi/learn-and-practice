# Exercises: Relative Pronouns

Fill in who, whom, which, that, or whose:

1. The man ___ car is parked outside is my uncle.
2. The book ___ you lent me was excellent.
3. She's the teacher ___ everyone respects.
4. It's the best movie ___ I've ever seen.
5. The person to ___ I spoke was helpful.

**Answers:** 1. whose 2. which/that 3. who/whom/that 4. that 5. whom
