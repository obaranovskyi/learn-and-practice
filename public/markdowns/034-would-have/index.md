# Would have (Past Hypothetical)

## Structure: would + have + past participle

## Uses

### Third Conditional
- If I **had known**, I **would have helped**.
- She **would have come** if you had invited her.

### Imaginary Past
- I **would have loved** to see that!
- That **would have been** amazing!

### Reported Speech
- She said she **would have helped**.

## Examples
- I **would have called** but I lost your number.
- He **wouldn't have believed** it.
