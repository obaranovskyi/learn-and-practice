# Exercises: All/Both/Whole/Every/Each

Fill in all, both, whole, every, or each:

1. ___ of my parents are teachers.
2. She read the ___ book in one day.
3. ___ student must bring their own lunch.
4. I visited ___ country in Europe.
5. ___ of the answers is correct.

**Answers:** 1. Both 2. whole 3. Every/Each 4. every 5. Each
