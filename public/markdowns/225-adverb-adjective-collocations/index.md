# Adverb + Adjective Collocations

Certain adverbs intensify certain adjectives.

## HIGHLY
- highly unlikely/probable
- highly successful/effective
- highly recommended
- highly skilled/qualified
- highly competitive

## DEEPLY
- deeply concerned/worried
- deeply moved/touched
- deeply disappointed
- deeply religious
- deeply rooted

## BITTERLY
- bitterly cold
- bitterly disappointed
- bitterly opposed

## WIDELY
- widely available
- widely known/believed
- widely used/accepted

## STRONGLY
- strongly recommend
- strongly believe
- strongly opposed

## FULLY
- fully aware/understand
- fully equipped/furnished
- fully booked

## PERFECTLY
- perfectly clear/normal
- perfectly acceptable
- perfectly reasonable

## Common Collocations
- ridiculously expensive
- utterly ridiculous
- absolutely essential
- seriously injured
- heavily influenced
