# Exercises: Fixed Expressions with Articles

Fill in the correct article (a, the, or Ø):

1. I go to work by ___ car.
2. I'll call you in ___ morning.
3. She made ___ mistake on the test.
4. We arrived just in ___ time.
5. On ___ other hand, it could work.

**Answers:** 1. Ø 2. the 3. a 4. Ø 5. the
