# Fixed Expressions with Articles

Many expressions have fixed article usage that must be memorized.

## No Article

- *by car/bus/train/plane* (transport)
- *on foot*
- *at night/noon/midnight*
- *by chance/accident/mistake*
- *in fact/general/particular*
- *at first/last*
- *on time* (punctual)
- *out of date/breath/work*

## Use "the"

- *in the morning/afternoon/evening*
- *at the moment*
- *in the end* (finally)
- *on the whole* (generally)
- *in the meantime*
- *on the other hand*
- *the day before yesterday*
- *the other day*
- *tell the truth/the time*

## Use "a/an"

- *have a look/rest/break*
- *make a mistake/decision/choice*
- *as a result*
- *once a week/month* (frequency)
- *a few/a little*
- *in a hurry*
- *What a pity/shame!*

## Contrasting Pairs

- *in time* (early enough) vs *on time* (punctual)
- *at the end* (location) vs *in the end* (finally)
