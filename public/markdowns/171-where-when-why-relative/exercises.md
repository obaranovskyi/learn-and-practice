# Exercises: Where/When/Why

Fill in where, when, or why:

1. That's the place ___ we first met.
2. I remember the time ___ phones didn't exist.
3. Do you know the reason ___ he quit?
4. Paris is the city ___ I want to live.
5. December is the month ___ it snows here.

**Answers:** 1. where 2. when 3. why 4. where 5. when
