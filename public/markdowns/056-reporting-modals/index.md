# Reporting with Modals

## Modal Changes

| Direct | Reported |
|--------|----------|
| can | could |
| will | would |
| may | might |
| must (obligation) | had to |
| shall | should/would |

## No Change
- could, would, should, might, ought to
- must (deduction) - optional change

## Examples
- "I can swim" → She said she **could** swim.
- "You must leave" → He said I **had to** leave.
- "It must be true" → She said it **must/had to** be true.
