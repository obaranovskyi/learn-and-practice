# Exercises: Possessive Nouns

Add the correct possessive form:

1. The (children) toys are everywhere.
2. My (parents) house is big.
3. (James) car is red.
4. The (women) department is upstairs.
5. This is (Tom and Sue) restaurant. (joint ownership)

**Answers:** 1. children's 2. parents' 3. James's/James' 4. women's 5. Tom and Sue's
