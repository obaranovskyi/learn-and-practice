# Dependent Prepositions

Dependent prepositions are fixed with specific words and must be memorized.

## Quick Reference Chart

### With OF
| Verbs | Adjectives | Nouns |
|-------|------------|-------|
| think of | afraid of | lack of |
| consist of | aware of | cause of |
| accuse of | tired of | example of |
| remind of | proud of | possibility of |

### With FOR
| Verbs | Adjectives | Nouns |
|-------|------------|-------|
| wait for | famous for | reason for |
| apply for | responsible for | need for |
| apologize for | ready for | demand for |
| search for | sorry for | respect for |

### With TO
| Verbs | Adjectives | Nouns |
|-------|------------|-------|
| listen to | similar to | answer to |
| belong to | married to | solution to |
| refer to | kind to | reaction to |
| look forward to | addicted to | damage to |

### With ABOUT
| Verbs | Adjectives |
|-------|------------|
| think about | worried about |
| talk about | excited about |
| complain about | curious about |
| dream about | happy about |

### With IN
| Verbs | Adjectives | Nouns |
|-------|------------|-------|
| believe in | interested in | increase in |
| succeed in | involved in | rise in |
| specialize in | disappointed in | fall in |

### With ON
| Verbs | Adjectives | Nouns |
|-------|------------|-------|
| depend on | keen on | effect on |
| concentrate on | based on | impact on |
| insist on | dependent on | influence on |
