# As...As (Equality)

Use **as...as** to show that two things are equal.

## Structure

**as + adjective/adverb + as**

- *She is **as tall as** her mother.*
- *He runs **as fast as** his brother.*
- *This is **as good as** the original.*

## With Nouns

**as + much/many + noun + as**
- *I have **as much money as** you.*
- *She has **as many books as** me.*

## Common Expressions

- *as soon as possible* (ASAP)
- *as far as I know*
- *as long as* (conditional)
- *as well as* (in addition to)

## Similes (Fixed Expressions)

- *as cold as ice*
- *as white as snow*
- *as light as a feather*
- *as quiet as a mouse*
- *as hard as rock*
- *as blind as a bat*
- *as busy as a bee*
- *as old as the hills*

## Twice/Three Times + As...As

- *This is **twice as expensive as** that.*
- *He earns **three times as much as** me.*
- *The new model is **half as heavy as** the old one.*
