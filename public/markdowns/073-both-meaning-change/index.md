# Verbs taking both (meaning change)

## Stop
- **Stop doing** = quit the activity
- **Stop to do** = pause in order to do

I **stopped smoking**. (quit)
I **stopped to smoke**. (paused to have a cigarette)

## Remember/Forget
- **Remember doing** = memory of past action
- **Remember to do** = remember before doing

I **remember locking** the door. (I recall doing it)
**Remember to lock** the door. (Don't forget!)

## Try
- **Try doing** = experiment
- **Try to do** = attempt with effort

**Try pressing** this button. (experiment)
I **tried to open** the door. (attempted)

## Regret
- **Regret doing** = feel sorry about past
- **Regret to say** = formal announcement
