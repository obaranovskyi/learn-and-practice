# Can / Could / Be able to

## Ability

### Present Ability
- I **can** swim.
- She **can't** drive.

### Past Ability
- I **could** swim when I was 5.
- He **couldn't** come yesterday.

### Future / Perfect Forms: Use "be able to"
- I **will be able to** help tomorrow.
- I **have been able to** finish it.

## Permission

### Can (informal)
- **Can** I use your phone?

### Could (more polite)
- **Could** I borrow your pen?

## Can vs Could vs Be able to

| Tense | Ability |
|-------|---------|
| Present | can / am able to |
| Past (general) | could |
| Past (specific occasion) | was able to |
| Future | will be able to |
