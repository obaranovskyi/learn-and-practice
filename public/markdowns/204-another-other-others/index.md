# Another, Other, The other, Others

## Another

**One more** (singular):
- *Can I have **another** cup of coffee?*
- *Let's try **another** way.*
- ***Another** student asked the same question.*

Also: **a different one**
- *Let's meet **another** day.*

## Other

**Additional** (with plural or uncountable):
- ***Other** people disagree.*
- *Do you have any **other** questions?*
- ***Other** information is available.*

## The Other

**Specific remaining one(s)**:

Singular (of two):
- *I have two books. One is red, **the other** is blue.*

Plural (specific group):
- *Some students passed. **The other** students failed.*
- *Where are **the others**?* (the remaining ones)

## Others (pronoun)

**Other people/things**:
- *Some people agree; **others** disagree.*
- *I'll take this one. You can have the **others**.*

## Summary

| Word | Form | Meaning |
|------|------|---------|
| another | singular | one more / a different one |
| other | + noun | additional |
| the other | singular | the remaining one (of two) |
| the others | plural | the remaining ones |
| others | pronoun | other people/things |

## Common Patterns

- *one... another... another*
- *one... the other* (of two)
- *some... others*
- *some... the others* (specific group)
