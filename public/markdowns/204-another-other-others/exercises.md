# Exercises: Another/Other/The other/Others

Fill in another, other, the other, or others:

1. I have two cats. One is black, ___ is white.
2. Would you like ___ piece of cake?
3. Some people like tea; ___ prefer coffee.
4. Do you have any ___ suggestions?
5. I'll take this book. Where are ___?

**Answers:** 1. the other 2. another 3. others 4. other 5. the others
