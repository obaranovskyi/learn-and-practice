# Subjunctive Mood

The subjunctive expresses wishes, demands, suggestions, or hypothetical situations.

## Present Subjunctive

Uses base form of verb (no -s for third person):
- *I suggest that he **go** to the meeting.* (NOT goes)
- *It's important that she **be** on time.* (NOT is)
- *They demand that he **leave** immediately.*

### Verbs Triggering Subjunctive
- suggest, recommend, propose
- demand, insist, request
- ask, require, order

### Adjectives Triggering Subjunctive
- important, essential, vital
- necessary, crucial, imperative

## Formulaic Subjunctive

Fixed expressions:
- *God **bless** you!*
- ***Be** that as it may...*
- *Long **live** the king!*
- ***Come** what may...*
- *If **need be**...*
- ***Suffice** it to say...*

## Were-Subjunctive

In hypothetical conditions:
- *If I **were** you...* (NOT was)
- *I wish I **were** taller.*
- *If she **were** here...*

## British vs American

- **American**: subjunctive more common
  - *I suggest that he go.*
- **British**: often use "should" instead
  - *I suggest that he should go.*
