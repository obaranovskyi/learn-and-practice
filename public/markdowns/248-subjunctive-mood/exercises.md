# Exercises: Subjunctive Mood

Choose correct form: 1. I suggest that he ___ (go/goes) now. 2. It's important that she ___ (be/is) careful. 3. If I ___ (was/were) rich... 4. They demand that he ___ (leave/leaves). 5. I wish I ___ (was/were) there.

**Answers:** 1. go 2. be 3. were 4. leave 5. were
