# Exercises: Reciprocal Pronouns

Choose each other/one another or a reflexive pronoun:

1. The twins look like ___. (mutual)
2. They introduced ___ at the party. (mutual)
3. She looked at ___ in the mirror.
4. The team members support ___.
5. They blamed ___ for the mistake. (mutual)

**Answers:** 1. each other 2. themselves (if each introduced self) or each other (mutual introductions) 3. herself 4. each other/one another 5. each other
