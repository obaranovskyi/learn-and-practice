# Exercises: Fronting

Rewrite with the underlined element fronted:

1. I really like **this song**.
2. A castle stood **on the hill**.
3. She is **very talented**.
4. I'll take **this one**, not that one.
5. She went **away** quickly.

**Answers:** 1. This song I really like. 2. On the hill stood a castle. 3. Very talented she is. 4. This one I'll take... 5. Away she went quickly.
