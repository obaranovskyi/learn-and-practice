# Should have / Ought to have

## Past Advice/Criticism
Talking about what was the right thing (but didn't happen).

### Structure: should/ought to + have + past participle

### Examples
- You **should have told** me! (but you didn't)
- I **shouldn't have eaten** so much. (but I did)
- She **ought to have called**. (but she didn't)

## Use
- Criticism of past actions
- Regret about past decisions
- Advice that wasn't followed

## With "Shouldn't"
- I **shouldn't have said** that. (regret)
- You **shouldn't have left** early. (criticism)
