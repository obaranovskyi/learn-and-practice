# Verbs followed by Gerund (-ing)

## Common verbs + -ing

### Enjoyment/Dislike
enjoy, like, love, hate, dislike, can't stand, don't mind

### Mental processes
consider, imagine, suggest, recommend

### Finishing/Avoiding
finish, stop, give up, quit, avoid, postpone, delay

### Other common
keep, practice, risk, deny, admit, miss

## Examples
- I **enjoy swimming**.
- She **suggested going** to the park.
- He **finished writing** the report.
- They **avoid eating** junk food.
