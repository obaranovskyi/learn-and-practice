# Passive Infinitives and Gerunds

## Passive Infinitive: to be + past participle
- She wants **to be promoted**.
- He expects **to be invited**.
- It needs **to be done**.

## Passive Gerund: being + past participle
- He hates **being told** what to do.
- She enjoys **being praised**.
- I remember **being taken** there as a child.

## Perfect Passive Forms
- Infinitive: **to have been done**
- Gerund: **having been done**

Examples:
- He claims **to have been robbed**.
- **Having been warned**, she left early.
