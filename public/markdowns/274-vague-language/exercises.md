# Exercises: Vague Language

Make less direct: 1. It costs $100. 2. There are 50 people. 3. I'm tired. 4. He's a doctor. 5. I bought books and games.

**Answers:** 1. It costs around/about $100. 2. There are about 50 people. 3. I'm kind of/sort of tired. 4. He's a doctor or something. 5. I bought books and stuff.
