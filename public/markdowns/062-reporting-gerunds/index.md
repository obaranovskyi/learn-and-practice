# Reporting with Gerunds

## Verbs + -ing
admit, deny, mention, recommend, suggest

- "I stole it." → He **admitted stealing** it.
- "I didn't do it." → She **denied doing** it.
- "Let's go out." → He **suggested going** out.

## Verbs + preposition + -ing
apologize for, insist on, accuse (sb) of, thank (sb) for

- "Sorry I'm late." → She **apologized for being** late.
- "You stole it!" → He **accused me of stealing** it.

## Examples
- "I broke the window." → He **admitted breaking** it.
- "I didn't lie." → She **denied lying**.
