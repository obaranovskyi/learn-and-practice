# Exercises: Less / The Least

Fill in less, least, fewer, or fewest:

1. This book is ___ interesting than that one.
2. She has the ___ experience of all.
3. There are ___ students this year.
4. I have ___ time than before.
5. That was the ___ helpful advice.

**Answers:** 1. less 2. least 3. fewer 4. less 5. least
