# Exercises: Compound-Complex Sentences

Identify the sentence type:

1. I like tea, and she likes coffee.
2. When I arrived, I called.
3. When I arrived, I called, but no one answered.
4. She sings.
5. Although it rained, we went out, and we had fun.

**Answers:** 1. Compound 2. Complex 3. Compound-complex 4. Simple 5. Compound-complex
