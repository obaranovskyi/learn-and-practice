# Exercises: Either...or

Complete with either...or:

1. You can ___ call me ___ text me.
2. ___ John ___ Mary is coming. (is/are)
3. ___ she leaves ___ I do.
4. Take ___ the red one ___ the blue one.
5. ___ you ___ he has to go. (has/have)

**Answers:** 1. either, or 2. Either, or, is 3. Either, or 4. either, or 5. Either, or, has
