# Exercises: Simple Sentences

Is it a simple sentence? (Yes/No)

1. The cat sleeps.
2. She sang and danced at the party.
3. I came, but she left.
4. John and Mary are friends.
5. When I arrived, he was sleeping.

**Answers:** 1. Yes 2. Yes (compound verb) 3. No (compound) 4. Yes (compound subject) 5. No (complex)
