# May / Might (Permission)

## May (Formal Permission)

### Asking Permission
- **May** I come in?
- **May** I ask a question?

### Giving Permission
- You **may** leave now.
- You **may not** use phones here.

## May vs Might (Permission)

- **Might** is rarely used for permission (old-fashioned)
