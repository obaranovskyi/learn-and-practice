# Exercises: Periods

Add periods where needed: 1. I live in New York 2. Dr Smith is here 3. She asked what time it is 4. We arrived at 3 pm 5. It was amazing

**Answers:** 1. I live in New York. 2. Dr. Smith is here. 3. No period (question) 4. We arrived at 3 p.m. 5. It was amazing. (or ! for emphasis)
