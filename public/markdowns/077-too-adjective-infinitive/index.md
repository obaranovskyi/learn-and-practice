# Too + adjective + infinitive

## Structure: too + adjective + to-infinitive

Means: so [adjective] that it's not possible

## Examples
- It's **too cold to swim**.
- She's **too young to drive**.
- The box is **too heavy to lift**.
- He spoke **too fast to understand**.

## With "for + object"
- The book is **too difficult for me to read**.
- It's **too late for us to go** out.

## Compare: too vs very
- It's **very** cold. (but possible)
- It's **too** cold. (impossible/inadvisable)
