# Common Fixed Expressions

Fixed expressions cannot be changed - learn them as units.

## Time Expressions
- **in time** (early enough): *We arrived in time for the movie.*
- **on time** (punctual): *The train was on time.*
- **at the moment**: *I'm busy at the moment.*
- **from time to time**: *occasionally*
- **once in a while**: *sometimes*

## Opinion & Agreement
- **in my opinion**: *In my opinion, it's too expensive.*
- **to be honest**: *To be honest, I don't like it.*
- **as a matter of fact**: *actually*
- **to tell the truth**: *To tell the truth, I forgot.*

## Emphasis
- **at all** (negative): *I don't like it at all.*
- **of course**: *Of course you can come.*
- **by all means**: *certainly, yes*
- **no doubt**: *certainly*

## Cause & Effect
- **as a result**: *He didn't study. As a result, he failed.*
- **due to**: *due to the weather*
- **thanks to**: *Thanks to you, we won.*

## Other Common Expressions
- by the way, in fact, at least
- in general, on the whole
- for example, such as
- in other words
- at first, at last, in the end
- on purpose, by accident/chance
- out of order, out of date
- on behalf of, in charge of
