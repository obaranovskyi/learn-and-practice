# Exercises: Past Participles

Identify the use:

1. The book was written in 1990. ___
2. I have seen that movie. ___
3. A broken heart is hard to mend. ___
4. Tired after work, she relaxed. ___
5. He had his phone fixed. ___

**Answers:** 1. passive voice 2. perfect tense 3. adjective 4. participle clause 5. causative
