# Verb + Noun Collocations

Certain verbs naturally go with certain nouns.

## MAKE
- make a decision/choice
- make a mistake/error
- make progress
- make an effort
- make money
- make a suggestion
- make a complaint
- make friends
- make noise
- make an appointment

## TAKE
- take a break/rest
- take a photo/picture
- take a shower/bath
- take notes
- take a look
- take advice
- take action
- take responsibility
- take a seat
- take a risk

## DO
- do homework/housework
- do business
- do damage
- do good/harm
- do a favor
- do research
- do exercise
- do your best

## HAVE
- have a conversation
- have a look
- have a meal
- have an idea
- have fun
- have a problem

## Common Mistakes
- ❌ make homework → ✅ do homework
- ❌ do a mistake → ✅ make a mistake
- ❌ do a photo → ✅ take a photo
