# Should / Ought to / Had better

## Should / Ought to
- General advice
- You **should** study more.

## Had better (stronger advice/warning)
- You**'d better** hurry! (or something bad will happen)
- You**'d better not** be late!

## Comparison

| Expression | Strength |
|------------|----------|
| Could | suggestion (weak) |
| Should/Ought to | advice |
| Had better | strong advice/warning |
| Must | obligation (strong) |
